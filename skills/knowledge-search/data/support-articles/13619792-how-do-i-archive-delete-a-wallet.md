1.  Tap "Assets" located within the bottom navigation bar
    
2.  Locate the name of the Wallet that you would like to Archive
    
3.  Tap on the wallet and then tap on the three vertical dots located at the upper right once inside the wallet
    
4.  Tap on "Archive Wallet"
    
5.  Confirm and tap on "Archive" again
    

NOTE: You can restore any archived wallets by tapping Menu > Settings > Restore Wallets.
