For additional privacy/security when doing crypto-to-crypto swaps in Edge, users have the ability to prefer that swaps are routed through decentralized exchanges (DEXs) rather than centralized exchanges (CEXs).

To change your default exchange settings in Edge to prefer DEXs follow these steps:

1.  Launch the app and login to your account
    
2.  Tap the Side Menu (≡) at the top right
    
3.  Tap Settings near the bottom
    
4.  Underneath Options, tap on Exchange Settings
    
5.  Tap the current selection under 'Preferred Exchange'
    
6.  Select ‘Prefer Decentralized’
