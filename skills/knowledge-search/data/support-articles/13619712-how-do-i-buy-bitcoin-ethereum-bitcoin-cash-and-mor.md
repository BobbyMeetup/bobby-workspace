You can purchase Bitcoin, Ethereum, Bitcoin Cash, Litecoin, and XRP through Edge providers! Most purchases are complete and delivered to your Edge account in less than 30 minutes.

1.  Tap on “Buy” at the bottom of the screen or the "Buy Crypto" tile.
    
2.  Select region and then tap "Credit and Debit Card"
    
3.  Select which asset you will be purchasing
    
4.  Enter the amount you'd like to purchase
    
5.  Follow the prompts from the selected partner.  
      
    ​
    

# All done!

# Keep in mind, some cards/banks decline cryptocurrency related transactions. Please reach out directly to the partner if you run into any verification issues.
