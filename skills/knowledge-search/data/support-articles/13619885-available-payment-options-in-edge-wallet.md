Edge Wallet integrates with multiple third-party providers, offering users a wide range of payment methods for buying and selling cryptocurrency. The availability of these payment options varies by region and provider.

# Available Payment Options in Edge Wallet

**1\. Bank & Wire Transfers**

-   **ACH Bank Transfer** (USA only)
    
    -   *Used for selling crypto via Kado, Paybis, and Banxa*
        
    -   Fees: ~1.5%
        
    -   Settlement: 2–3 business days
        
-   **Bank Wire Transfer** (USA only)
    
    -   *Used for buying crypto via Kado*
        
    -   Fees: ~2%
        
    -   Settlement: ~5 minutes
        
    -   Limit: $10,000
        
-   **Interac e-Transfer** (Canada)
    
    -   *Supported by Banxa for Canadian users*
        
    -   Fees: ~4%–5%
        
    -   Settlement: 1–2 business days
        
-   **iDEAL** (Netherlands)
    
    -   *Supported by Banxa for users in the Netherlands*
        
    -   Fees: ~4%
        
    -   Settlement: Instant to 24 hours
        
-   **PIX** (Brazil)
    
    -   *Available through Paybis for instant local bank payments*
        
    -   Fees: ~3%
        
    -   Settlement: 5 minutes to 24 hours
        
-   **SEPA & UK Faster Payments**
    
    -   *Used by MoonPay, Banxa, Simplex, and Paybis in supported European regions*
        
    -   Fees: ~1.55%
        
    -   Settlement: 1–2 business days
        
-   **SPEI** (Mexico)
    
    -   *Available through select providers for instant transfers in Mexico*
        
    -   Fees: ~3.5%
        
    -   Settlement: 5 minutes to 24 hours
        

**2\. Credit/Debit Cards** (Various Regions)

-   Supported by MoonPay, Paybis, Banxa, and Simplex
    
-   Brands: Visa, MasterCard, Maestro
    
-   Fees: ~3.5%–5%
    
-   Settlement: Instant (subject to verification)
    

**3\. Apple Pay & Google Pay** (Various Regions)

-   Supported on mobile devices through MoonPay, Paybis, Banxa, and Simplex
    
-   Fees: ~5%–7%
    
-   Settlement: 10–30 minutes
    

**4\. ATM (LibertyX)** (USA only)

-   Users in the U.S. can purchase crypto with cash using the LibertyX network of ATMs
    
-   Requires app verification and eligible partner locations
    
-   Fees: ~8%
    
-   Settlement: Instant to 1 hour
    

**5\. PayPal** (Various Regions)

-   Available in select regions via MoonPay
    
-   Fees: ~5%
    
-   Settlement: 5 minutes to 24 hours
    

**6\. Revolut** (Various Regions)

-   Supported by Paybis for users with Revolut accounts in approved countries
    
-   Fees: ~4%
    
-   Settlement: 1–2 business days
    

**7\. Venmo** (USA only)

-   Available through MoonPay in select regions
    
-   Fees: ~4.5%
    
-   Settlement: Instant to 24 hours
    

# Payment Provider Integration

Edge Wallet supports the following providers, each offering different payment options:

-   **Banxa**
    
-   **Kado**
    
-   **LibertyX** (ATM/cash purchases only)
    
-   **MoonPay**
    
-   **Paybis**
    
-   **Simplex**
    

# \*Important Notes

-   *Payment method availability varies by country and region.*
    
-   *Some providers may restrict services in specific U.S. states or global regions.*
    
-   *Always verify identity (KYC) requirements and transaction limits based on the provider used.*
