Transferring your cryptocurrency from Binance to Edge Wallet is straightforward. Follow these steps to ensure a secure and successful transfer.​

---

# Step 1: Prepare Your Edge Wallet

1.  **Open Edge Wallet**: Launch the Edge Wallet app on your device.
    
2.  **Access Assets**: Tap on the **Assets** button located at the bottom of the screen.
    
3.  **Select Cryptocurrency**: Choose the cryptocurrency you wish to receive (e.g., Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT). If it's not visible, use the search bar to find or enable it.
    
4.  **Get Receiving Address**: Tap **Request** or **Receive** to display your wallet's receiving address. Copy this address for use in Binance.​
    

---

# Step 2: Initiate Withdrawal on Binance

1.  **Log In**: Access your Binance account via the app or website.
    
2.  **Navigate to Wallet**: Hover over the account icon in the upper right corner and select **\[Wallet\]**.
    
3.  **Select Withdraw**: Click on **\[Withdraw\]** and choose the cryptocurrency you want to transfer.
    
4.  **Enter Details**:
    
    -   **Paste Address**: In the withdrawal address field, paste the receiving address you copied from Edge Wallet.
        
    -   **Select Network**: Choose the correct network that matches your Edge Wallet (e.g., ERC20 for Ethereum, BEP20 for Binance Smart Chain).
        
    -   **Specify Amount**: Enter the amount you wish to withdraw.​
        

---

# Step 3: Confirm and Complete the Withdrawal

1.  **Review Details**: Double-check all information, ensuring the address and network are correct.
    
2.  **Security Verification**: Complete any required security steps, such as two-factor authentication (2FA).
    
3.  **Email Confirmation**: Binance will send a confirmation email. Click the link provided to finalize the withdrawal.​
    

---

# Step 4: Await Transfer Completion

-   **Processing Time**: The transfer may take a few minutes to complete, depending on network congestion.
    
-   **Verification**: You can monitor the transaction status within Edge Wallet or by using a blockchain explorer with the transaction ID.​
    

---

# Important Tips

-   **Network Selection**: Always ensure the network selected on Binance matches the one supported by your Edge Wallet. Sending funds via an incorrect network can result in loss of funds.
    
-   **Test Transfers**: If you're transferring a large amount, consider sending a small test amount first to confirm everything is set up correctly.
    
-   **Support**: If you encounter any issues, don't hesitate to contact Edge Wallet support for assistance.​
    

---

By following these steps, you can securely transfer your cryptocurrency from Binance to your Edge Wallet.
