# Celo (CELO) Network – Fast Mobile-Friendly Blockchain

Celo focuses on bringing blockchain to mobile users with extremely low fees and fast transactions.

## How CELO Fees Work

-   **Gas fees (gwei x gas limit)** paid in **CELO** tokens.
    
-   Like Ethereum, but much cheaper and optimized for fast confirmations.
    

Edge Wallet auto-calculates the best gas price based on current Celo network conditions.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – Fastest confirmation
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Balanced
    
-   **Low** – Cheapest option
    
-   **Custom** – Full manual setting available
    

---

## Average Cost

Usually **$0.001 to $0.005 USD** per transaction.

---

## Why It Matters

Celo brings blockchain to smartphones at incredible speeds and costs. Edge handles fee optimization automatically so you get the best price without lifting a finger.
