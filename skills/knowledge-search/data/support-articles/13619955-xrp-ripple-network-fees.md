# XRP (Ripple) Network – Fast and Predictable

XRP is known for its speed and incredibly low fees, designed specifically for payments and financial institutions.

## How XRP Fees Work

Fees are:

-   **Minimal and fixed**
    
-   **Based on a dynamic base fee** adjusted by the network  
    Paid in **XRP**.
    

Edge automatically handles the tiny fee calculation for you.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **One setting** – XRP transactions are fast and cheap by design.
    

---

## Average Cost

Usually **$0.0001 to $0.001 USD**—even large payments cost almost nothing.

---

## Why It Matters

XRP’s fast and cheap transactions make it ideal for quick money movement. Edge Wallet makes XRP transfers seamless without any fee guesswork.
