**Why Was My Bitrefill Transaction Declined?**

If your Bitrefill transaction was declined, it may be due to payment issues, account limitations, or security measures. Here’s a quick breakdown of what might’ve gone wrong and how to resolve it:

**Common Reasons**

1.  **Insufficient Funds:** Crypto wallet or payment method doesn’t cover the purchase
    
2.  **Spending Limits:** You’ve hit Bitrefill’s daily or monthly transaction limits
    
3.  **High-Risk Activity:** The transaction was flagged by Bitrefill’s security system
    
4.  **Restricted Vendors:** Some purchases may be blocked based on vendor category
    
5.  **Geo-Restrictions:** Services may not be available in your region
    

**Recommended Actions**

1.  Double-check your wallet balance or payment method
    
2.  Stay within account or transaction limits
    
3.  Avoid restricted or unsupported services
    
4.  Make sure you're using a supported payment method
    
5.  Monitor your inbox for Bitrefill alerts or verification requests
    
6.  [**Contact Bitrefill Support**](https://help.bitrefill.com/hc/en-us)**: https://help.bitrefill.com/hc/en-us**
