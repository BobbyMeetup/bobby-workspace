# FIO Protocol (FIO) – Simplifying Crypto Transactions

FIO Protocol improves crypto usability by letting users send crypto to human-readable FIO addresses instead of long public keys.

## How FIO Fees Work

-   **Transaction fees** paid in **FIO tokens**.
    
-   Some operations (like sending a FIO request) have a flat small fee.
    

Edge handles the FIO transaction fee calculations automatically for you.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **Standard FIO fees** – No need for manual adjustments.
    

---

## Average Cost

Generally **$0.01 to $0.05 USD** depending on the FIO service action (e.g., sending, requesting payment).

---

## Why It Matters

FIO makes crypto easier for everyone by replacing wallet addresses with simple names. Edge Wallet integrates FIO functionality while keeping transaction fees super affordable and automatic.
