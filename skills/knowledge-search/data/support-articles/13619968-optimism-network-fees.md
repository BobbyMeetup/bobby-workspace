# Optimism Network – Simple, Affordable Ethereum Scaling

Optimism is another major Ethereum Layer 2, focused on speed and lower fees. Transactions settle on Ethereum but cost much less.

## How Optimism Fees Work

Fees are based on:

-   **Gas price (gwei)** times
    
-   **Amount of gas used**
    

Edge calculates optimized fee suggestions for you automatically.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – Faster confirmation
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Balanced fee
    
-   **Low** – Cheapest, slower confirmation
    
-   **Custom** – Full manual control for gas settings
    

---

## Average Cost

Usually around **$0.01 to $0.05 USD** per transaction.

---

## Why It Matters

Optimism makes Ethereum accessible without high fees. Edge handles the real-time adjustments so you just pick your speed and go.

# Polygon (Matic) Network Fees – Ultra-Cheap and Fast

Polygon is a highly popular Ethereum-compatible network with extremely low fees and very fast block times.

## How Polygon Fees Work

-   **Gas price (in gwei)**
    
-   **Network congestion**
    

Fees are still paid in MATIC token but are much cheaper than Ethereum.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – Fastest confirmation
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Smart middle ground
    
-   **Low** – Cheapest, slow confirmation
    
-   **Custom** – Full control over gas settings
    

---

## Average Cost

Typically around **$0.002 to $0.01 USD** per transaction — fractions of a cent!

---

## Why It Matters

Polygon is perfect for cheap everyday use, gaming, NFTs, and DeFi. Edge Wallet simplifies picking the right fee so you stay fast without overpaying
