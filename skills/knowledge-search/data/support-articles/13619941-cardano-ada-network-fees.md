# Cardano (ADA) Network – Stable and Predictable

Cardano aims for reliability and stability, using a unique transaction fee formula.

## How ADA Fees Work

-   **Fixed minimum fee + size-based fee**  
    Paid in **ADA tokens**.
    

Edge handles the calculation behind the scenes for you.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **Automatic calculation** — No manual settings needed.
    

---

## Average Cost

Usually **$0.10 to $0.20 USD** per transaction.

---

## Why It Matters

Cardano keeps fees predictable even as the network grows. Edge ensures your ADA transactions are fast and cost-effective without any complicated settings.
