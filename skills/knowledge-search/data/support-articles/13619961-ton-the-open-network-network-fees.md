# TON (The Open Network) – Speed and Simplicity

TON is a high-speed blockchain originally developed by Telegram’s team, now decentralized and focused on fast, affordable transactions.

## How TON Fees Work

TON fees are based on:

-   **Transaction size** (in bytes)
    
-   **Network congestion**  
    Fees are paid in **TON tokens**.
    

Edge automatically estimates the ideal fee for your TON transaction based on network activity and transaction size.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – Fastest confirmation
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Balanced cost and speed
    
-   **Low** – Cheapest option, slightly slower
    
-   **Custom** – Manually set your own fee if you prefer
    

---

## Average Cost

A TON transaction usually costs around **$0.001 to $0.005 USD** — extremely cheap.

---

## Why It Matters

TON is designed for ultra-fast, low-cost transactions, perfect for daily use. With Edge, you don’t have to worry about overpaying—smart fee estimates keep it simple and efficient.
