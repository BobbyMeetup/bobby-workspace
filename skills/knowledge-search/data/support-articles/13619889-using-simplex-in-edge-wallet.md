# Getting Started

1.  **Open Edge Wallet:** Launch the Edge Wallet app on your device.
    
2.  **Navigate to Buy/Sell:** Tap on the "Buy" or "Sell" option.
    
3.  **Select Your Region:** Tap "Select Region" and choose your country/state.
    
4.  **Select Simplex:** Choose Simplex as your service provider.
    
5.  **Enter Details:** Input the required information and follow the prompts to complete your transaction.
