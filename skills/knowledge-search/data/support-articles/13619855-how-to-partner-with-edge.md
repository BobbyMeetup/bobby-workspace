Edge is a leading mobile cryptocurrency app committed to delivering secure and user-friendly crypto experiences. If you're interested in partnering with us, follow this guide to submit a compelling partnership proposal. We receive many inquiries, so ensuring your proposal aligns with our criteria will help us evaluate it efficiently. Here's how to get started.

# Step 1: Identify Your Partnership Type

Determine the type of partnership you’re proposing. We consider the following categories:

-   **Fiat Exchange**: Integrating fiat on/off-ramp services.
    
-   **Swap Exchange**: Adding centralized (CEX) or decentralized (DEX) exchange capabilities.
    
-   **Asset Integration**: Supporting a new cryptocurrency asset.
    
-   **Staking Services**: Offering staking opportunities for assets.
    

# Step 2: Prepare Your Proposal

Tailor your proposal to the specific partnership type by addressing the relevant criteria below. Be clear, concise, and highlight the unique value your partnership brings to Edge users.

## For Fiat Exchange Partnerships

To propose a fiat exchange integration:

-   **Verify**: Provide details about your regulatory compliance, licenses, and operational history to demonstrate credibility.
    
-   **Highlight Unique Offerings**: Specify payment methods or regions you support that Edge does not currently cover.
    
-   **Explain Your Value**: Clearly articulate why integrating your service would enhance the Edge user experience and benefit our platform.
    

## For Swap Exchange Partnerships

Edge has deep swap support, particularly through DEX aggregators like LiFi, so new partnerships must offer unique value:

-   **Centralized Exchanges (CEX)**: We are unlikely to add new CEX partners unless they provide exceptional, differentiated features. Explain what sets your CEX apart.
    
-   **Decentralized Exchanges (DEX)**: If proposing a DEX, demonstrate how your platform offers liquidity or features not available through our current DEX partners or aggregators. Highlight any innovative advantages.
    

## For Asset Integration

If proposing a new cryptocurrency asset:

-   **Confirm Swap Support**: Verify whether the asset is supported for swaps on any of our existing exchange partners.
    
-   **Provide Details**: Include information about the asset’s use case, market traction, and why it would appeal to Edge users.
    

## For Staking Services

We welcome staking proposals that offer compelling value to our users:

-   **Showcase Unique Benefits**: Explain why your staking service is a strategic fit for Edge users, including any innovative features or significant user demand.
    
-   **Provide Supporting Data**: Include metrics or evidence that demonstrate the value of your staking offering.
    

# Step 3: Submit Your Proposal

Send your proposal via email to **[info@edge.app](mailto:info@edge.app)**. Include the following:

-   **Partnership Type**: Clearly state whether you’re proposing a fiat exchange, swap exchange, asset integration, or staking partnership.
    
-   **Responses to Criteria**: Address the specific criteria outlined above for your partnership type.
    
-   **Additional Value**: Share any other details that showcase how your partnership will enhance the Edge platform and benefit our users.
    

# Step 4: Await Our Review

Our team will evaluate your proposal to determine if it aligns with our strategic goals and user needs. Due to the volume of inquiries, we may not respond to every submission. However, we appreciate your effort and interest in partnering with Edge.

# Tips for a Successful Proposal

-   **Be Specific**: Avoid vague pitches. Provide concrete details about your service and its benefits.
    
-   **Research Our Platform**: Familiarize yourself with Edge’s existing features and partners to ensure your proposal fills a gap.
    
-   **Focus on Users**: Emphasize how your partnership will improve the experience for Edge’s community.
    

By following these steps, you’ll create a strong proposal that helps us assess the potential for a successful partnership. We look forward to exploring opportunities that align with our mission to empower users with secure and seamless crypto solutions.
