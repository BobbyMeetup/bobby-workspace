**Why Was My LibertyX Transaction Declined?**

If your LibertyX crypto ATM transaction was declined, it may be due to account verification, payment method issues, or transaction limits. Here’s a quick breakdown of what might’ve gone wrong and how to fix it:

**Common Reasons**

1.  **Verification Incomplete:** ID or phone verification not completed
    
2.  **Payment Issues:** Insufficient funds or declined debit card
    
3.  **Transaction Limits:** Daily or monthly limits exceeded
    
4.  **Security Review:** Transaction flagged as high-risk or suspicious
    

**Recommended Actions**

1.  Complete all required verification in the LibertyX app
    
2.  Ensure your card has sufficient funds and is supported
    
3.  Review LibertyX account limits before retrying
    
4.  Check your email (and spam) for messages from LibertyX
    
5.  Retry the transaction once all issues are resolved
    
6.  Contact LibertyX Support using the chat bubble on this page: [https://libertyx.zendesk.com/hc/en-us](https://libertyx.zendesk.com/hc/en-us)
