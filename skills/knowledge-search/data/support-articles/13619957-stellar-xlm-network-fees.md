# Stellar (XLM) Network – Ultra-Low and Instant

Stellar is optimized for fast, cheap payments across borders, with one of the lowest fee structures in the crypto space.

## How XLM Fees Work

Fees are:

-   **Flat and minimal**
    
-   **Based on a tiny fixed base fee** (currently 0.00001 XLM)  
    Paid in **XLM**.
    

Edge handles fee estimation automatically—you barely notice the cost.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **One setting** – Stellar’s network design makes multiple presets unnecessary. Fast and cheap by default.
    

---

## Average Cost

Usually **$0.000001 to $0.000005 USD** per transaction—effectively free.

---

## Why It Matters

Stellar’s near-free fees are perfect for payments, remittances, and microtransactions. Edge lets you send XLM instantly without worrying about gas prices or surges.
