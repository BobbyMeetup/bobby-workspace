If you’re interested in joining the Edge team, we’d love to help you find out about current opportunities! When Edge is hiring, we post all available job listings on our official LinkedIn company page. You can check for openings by visiting:

**[https://www.linkedin.com/company/edgeapp/](https://www.linkedin.com/company/edgeapp/)**

Be sure to follow our LinkedIn page to stay updated on new postings and company news. If no positions are listed, feel free to check back later—our team is always growing, and new opportunities may arise!
