**Navigation Access:** You can reach this screen at any time by tapping **Exchange** from the bottom navigation bar. **\*Swipe your finger down to see the full navigation bar and up to minimize it.**

The Exchange tab lets you swap one cryptocurrency for another directly inside the app, using integrated third-party providers.

**1\. Select Source Wallet**  
Choose the crypto wallet you want to exchange from (e.g., BTC).

**2\. Select Receiving Wallet**  
Choose the destination wallet (e.g., ETH) to receive the converted funds.

**3\. Review the Quote**  
Once both wallets are selected, Edge will display:

-   The estimated amount you’ll receive
    
-   Any provider fees
    
-   Network fee details  
    Note: The amount shown is an estimate. Actual received funds may be slightly lower depending on network conditions.
    

**4\. Change Provider**  
Tap "Powered by Thorchain" (or other listed provider) to see available exchange partners and switch if desired.

**5\. Slide to Confirm**  
When you're ready, slide the button to finalize the exchange.
