Spot wallet is sunsetting their wallet app on April 30th 2024, and has provided instructions ([here](https://help.spot-wallet.com/en/articles/9031893-spot-will-shut-down-on-april-30-2024)) to export assets. Before April 30, you may simply create an Edge account and send funds over. Afterwards, it would require importing your 12-word seed phrase from Spot wallet to restore access to each asset you were holding within their app. Be extra careful to NOT expose these 12 words to any unknown or insecure (phishing) apps/websites! Safely import with Edge to migrate and consolidate your crypto assets into one, multi-chain, self-custodial wallet and exchange routing tool.

To do this:

1.  [Download Edge](https://edge.app/get-started/) here or from your mobile app store.
    
2.  C**reate a new account** by choosing a username and a password.  
    Edge is client-side encrypted, so your wallet will remain non-custodial, just like with Spot wallet! We have no access or way to help you recover lost login credentials. Safely back-up this information! For detailed account creation steps, see this guide: [How do I create an account?](https://support.edge.app/hc/en-us/articles/7019038717339-How-do-I-create-an-account) *Note: the last step of creating an Edge account requires a minimum of one new wallet before your Edge account is finally created, and you can begin the import process. You can add or remove wallets in your Edge account at anytime later.*
    
3.  Once you are fully logged into your new account, tap on 'View Assets' near the top, or use the 'Assets' tab near the bottom to view the wallets currently in your account.
    
4.  Tap the green "**+**" symbol near the top, to the right of the word 'Wallets'
    
5.  Select all cryptocurrencies that you're holding in Spot, and tap **Next**
    
6.  Tap **Import Wallets**, to proceed (you may rename these wallets now or later)
    
7.  Now, enter your 12 word mnemonic phrase with 100% accuracy. **Be sure:**
    
    \- that all words are lowercase and have one space between each word  
    \- there's no extra space at the beginning or end  
    \- to take your time, and review your input for accuracy
    
8.  After reviewing your input, Tap **Next**
    
9.  Finally, Tap **Done 🎉**
    
10.  Reach out to our industry leading support team if you have any questions at all, using the chat bubble in the lower right or the email below.
     

To protect your crypto for a lifetime and beyond, please consider having a quick read through this article, too: [How to maximize my Edge account security?](https://support.edge.app/hc/en-us/articles/7070265416347)
