# Coreum (COREUM) – Next-Gen Enterprise Blockchain

Coreum focuses on high scalability for business and DeFi use cases.

## How Coreum Fees Work

-   **Gas model** similar to Cosmos
    
-   **Paid in COREUM tokens**.
    

Edge calculates optimal gas usage per transaction type.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High**
    
-   **Optimal (default)**
    
-   **Low**
    
-   **Custom**
    

---

## Average Cost

About **$0.01 to $0.03 USD**.

---

## Why It Matters

Coreum is designed for mass-scale apps, and Edge ensures your fees stay light, fast, and appropriate for both small and large transactions.
