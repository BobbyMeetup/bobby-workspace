Edge Wallet integrates with MoonPay, enabling users to purchase and sell cryptocurrencies seamlessly within the app.

# Accepted Payment Methods

**Buy Crypto:**

-   Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, Maestro) *~5-7% • Settlement: ~10-30 Minutes*
    
-   Apple Pay (iOS devices) *~5-7% • Settlement: ~10-30 Minutes*
    
-   Google Pay (Android devices) *~5-7% • Settlement: ~10-30 Minutes*
    
-   PayPal (Available in select regions) *~5% • Settlement: 5 MIN -24 Hours*
    
-   Venmo (Available in select regions) *~5% • Settlement: 5 MIN -24 Hours*  
      
      
    ​
    

**Sell Crypto:**

-   Visa Direct & Mastercard Direct Payouts *~5-7% • Settlement: ~10-30 Minutes*
    
-   PayPal (Available in select regions) *~5% • Settlement: 5 MIN -24 Hours*
    
-   Venmo (Available in select regions) *~5% • Settlement: 5 MIN -24 Hours*  
      
    ​
    

*\*Note: Payment method availability is subject to change and also varies by country or region.*

# Supported Regions

MoonPay's services are accessible in over 160 countries. However, certain regions are restricted due to regulatory reasons.

**Supported Regions Include:**

North America: United States\*, Canada  
Europe: United Kingdom, Germany, France, Spain, Italy, Netherlands, and more  
Asia-Pacific: Australia, New Zealand, South Korea, Japan, and others  
Latin America: Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, Chile, and more  
Africa: South Africa, Nigeria, Kenya, and others

**\*Restricted U.S. States and Territories:**

-   New York
    
-   Texas
    
-   Louisiana
    
-   U.S. Virgin Islands
    
-   American Samoa
    
-   Northern Mariana Islands

# Getting Started

Open Edge Wallet: Launch the Edge Wallet app on your device.  
Navigate to Buy/Sell: Tap on the "Buy" or "Sell" option.  
Select Your Region: Tap "Select Region" and choose your country/state.  
Select MoonPay: Choose MoonPay as your service provider.  
Enter Details: Input the required information and follow the prompts to complete your transaction.
