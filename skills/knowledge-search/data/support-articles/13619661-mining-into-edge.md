We welcome mining into Edge however we do have a few tips that need to be followed to create the best experience.

Tips for mining into Edge:

-   If you're using a pool set the payout frequency to weekly or longer. The Edge app may not function properly if many payouts are going to the same address in a short period of time such as many per day.
    
-   Periodically you will want to perform a consolidation. Send your max amount to yourself which will move the funds to a new address in the same wallet. This will prevent any syncing issues.
    
    -   You can do so by copying your own address and then sending the max amount to yourself.
        
        -   -   Tap into you BTC wallet.
                
            -   Tap "Receive" and copy
                
            -   Go back one screen and then tap "Send"
                
            -   Paste your address and then send MAX.
