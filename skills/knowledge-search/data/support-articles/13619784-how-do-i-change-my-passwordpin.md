With Edge wallet changing you, password/pin is a simple process.

1.  Go to the setting. Tap the 3 horizontal lines in the top right corner
    
2.  Tap settings
    
3.  Tap change Password (same steps for change PIN) and enter your password
    
4.  Enter what you would like to change it to and then confirm.
