Edge accounts are not tied to an identity.

When you create an account only a username, password, and 4 digit PIN are asked which is encrypted and known only to you. Not even Edge knows this information.

Edge never asks for your name, phone number, email, or address so no personal information is ever tied to your Edge account.

NOTE: Edge partners used for buying and selling may require KYC or verification. This information is never stored with Edge only with that partner.
