# Dash (DASH) Network – Instant and Cheap

Dash is designed for **speed and low fees**, with features like InstantSend for nearly-instant payments.

## How Dash Fees Work

Dash uses the UTXO model like Bitcoin, so fees depend on:

-   **Transaction size in bytes**
    
-   **Current network congestion**
    

Edge queries Dash nodes to recommend the best fee for your transaction.

**InstantSend transactions** may cost slightly more but confirm almost instantly.

---

## Fee Options You Can Choose

-   **High** – Fastest confirmation, great if you're using InstantSend
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Balanced speed and cost
    
-   **Low** – Cheapest fee, slower confirmation
    
-   **Custom** – Full control for advanced users
    

To adjust: Start a send → Tap **fee settings gear** → Pick your preset or set a custom fee.

---

## Average Cost

A standard Dash transaction costs around **$0.002 to $0.005 USD**.  
Even InstantSend is extremely affordable.

---

## Why It Matters

Dash offers blazing-fast and private transactions. Edge Wallet keeps it simple, automatically suggesting the best fees for speed or savings based on your needs.
