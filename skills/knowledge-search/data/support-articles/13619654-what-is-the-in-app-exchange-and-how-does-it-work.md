Edge's in-app Exchange gives users the ability to swap between different digital assets directly within their account. Edge checks your order across multiple exchanges and presents the user with the best rate.

To use this feature follow the instructions below:

1) Tap "Exchange" located within the bottom navigation bar

2) Select the source wallet you'd like to convert from

3) Enter the amount

4) Choose the wallet you'd like to receive into (if you don't have a wallet yet for the crypto you'd like to receive you can create it from the wallet picker)

5) Tap on Next

6) After the app identifies the best trade, use the slider to confirm the trade

Each trade requires the funding transaction to confirm on its blockchain before our partners complete the trade. This usually only takes a few minutes but could take longer if there are a lot of other pending transactions in the network.

Your trades are automatically tagged with the exchange partner name and details about the trade.
