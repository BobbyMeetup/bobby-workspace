**Why Was My Simplex Transaction Declined?**

If your transaction via Simplex didn’t go through, it may be due to security filters, payment issues, or verification problems.

**Common Reasons**

1.  **Payment Issues:** Insufficient funds, incorrect card details, or no 3D Secure
    
2.  **Verification Incomplete:** Missing ID or payment method confirmation
    
3.  **Bank Restrictions:** Crypto purchases may be blocked or marked as cash advances
    
4.  **Security Flag:** Fraud detection system may auto-decline suspicious activity
    

**Recommended Actions**

1.  Double-check card details and verification status
    
2.  Try another payment method
    
3.  Contact your bank
    
4.  Check your email (including spam) for Simplex verification requests
    
5.  [**Contact Simplex Support**](https://www.simplex.com/SUPPORT)**: https://www.simplex.com/SUPPORT**
