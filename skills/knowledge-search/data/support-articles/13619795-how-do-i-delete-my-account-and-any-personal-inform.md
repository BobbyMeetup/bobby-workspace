Edge does not store personal information that ties any identity to that account. When you create an Edge account no information such as a name, address, phone number, or email is ever collected. **We store zero information so that all Edge accounts are private.**

To delete your account tap Menu from inside the account and then tap on Settings. Once you're in settings tap "Account Settings" and then "Delete Account."
