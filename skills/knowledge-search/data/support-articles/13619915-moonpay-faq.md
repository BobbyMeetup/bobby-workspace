**What is MoonPay?**

  
MoonPay is a fintech company that enables web and mobile developers to let their users purchase virtual currencies using their everyday credit card. In Edge, users can purchase crypto with Apple Pay and credit cards.

**Where can I use MoonPay?**  
  
Edge users in the EU, South Korea, Japan, Canada, Mexico, and others, can use MoonPay to purchase crypto. Please tap "Buy" in app to check availability.  
  
​

**How do I buy with MoonPay?  
**

To buy cryptocurrencies, simply go into your Edge account and select Buy on the bottom menu bar.

Set the location to the country where you are located and you will find a list of services that are supported in your country.  
  
**What payment methods are supported by MoonPay?  
**  
​

MoonPay supports debit and credit cards and is the only Edge partner that supports purchasing with Apple Pay.
