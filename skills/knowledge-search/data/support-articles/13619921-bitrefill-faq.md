**What is Bitrefill?**

Bitrefill provides products and services that allow anyone to live on cryptocurrency more easily. Bitrefill.com provides a catalog of gift cards and prepaid mobile refills that can be purchased using Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies worldwide. You only need to provide an email address to receive a receipt, voucher codes if applicable, and customer service.

**Where can I use Bitrefill?**  
  
Bitrefill can be used anywhere in the world. Please go [here](https://support.edge.app/hc/en-us/articles/7075778781211) for a comprehensive list of countries and supported payment methods in Edge.  
  
​

**How soon are the gift cards delivered?  
**  
Most purchases with bitcoin are delivered instantly as soon as the transaction is seen on the network. Other currencies may require [network confirmations](https://www.bitrefill.com/faq/?hl=en#when-is-order-delivered) before the gift card is sent to your email.  
  
**What is the maximum limit?  
**  
There are daily limits depending on the gift card category, with some reaching as high as $10,000.
