**Buying Bitcoin with LibertyX in Your Edge Wallet**

LibertyX allows you to purchase Bitcoin (BTC) with cash or a debit card at ATMs, convenience stores, and debit kiosks across the United States. Here’s how to get started:

**How to Buy BTC:**

1.  Open Edge and tap the "Buy" tab from the bottom navigation bar
    
2.  Select LibertyX
    
3.  Enter your zip code or tap "Near Me" to find a nearby LibertyX location
    
4.  Tap on a location close to you and follow the on-screen instructions to initiate a purchase
    

-   LibertyX requests location permissions to help you find nearby locations; however, this permission is not required to place an order.
    

**LibertyX Locations:**

-   LibertyX is available across the United States.
    
-   You can search for locations within the LibertyX plugin in your Edge app.
    
-   Alternatively, you can find locations here: [https://libertyx.com/a/buy-bitcoin/location-map](https://libertyx.com/a/buy-bitcoin/location-map)
    

**What is LibertyX?**

-   LibertyX operates a large network of local stores where you can purchase Bitcoin in person.
    
-   In Edge, you can use their platform to buy Bitcoin with cash or a debit card.
    

**Supported Cryptocurrencies:**

-   LibertyX currently supports only Bitcoin (BTC)
    

**Fees and Settlement:**

-   Fees: 3-8%
    
-   Settlement: Instant
    

**Purchase Limits:**

-   Minimum Purchase: $1
    
-   Daily Maximum: $200-$3000
    

**Payment Methods:**

-   LibertyX locations may have either a kiosk for debit cards, or cash retailers for cash transactions.
    
-   Credit cards are not accepted.
    

**Order Status and Support:**

-   For questions about your order or its status, contact LibertyX directly at support@libertyx.com
