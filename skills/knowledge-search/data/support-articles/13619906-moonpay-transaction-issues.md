**Why Was My MoonPay Transaction Declined?**

If your MoonPay transaction was declined, it’s usually due to card issues, verification problems, or security filters. Here’s a quick overview of what might’ve gone wrong and how to fix it:

**Common Reasons**

1.  **Card Issues:** Insufficient funds, incorrect details, or card not 3D Secure-enabled
    
2.  **Verification Incomplete:** ID or payment method not fully verified
    
3.  **Bank Restrictions:** Some banks block crypto transactions or flag them as risky
    
4.  **Purchase Limits:** You may have hit daily or weekly transaction limits
    
5.  **Security Flag:** Suspicious activity may trigger an automatic block
    

**Recommended Actions**

1.  Try a different card or payment method
    
2.  Complete all verification steps
    
3.  Contact your bank to approve the payment
    
4.  Check your email (including spam) for MoonPay updates
    
5.  [**Contact MoonPay Support**](https://support.moonpay.com/)**:** [https://support.moonpay.com/](https://support.moonpay.com/)
