# Ravencoin (RVN) Network – Asset Transfers on Bitcoin Tech

Ravencoin is built for issuing and transferring digital assets, with low and predictable transaction fees.

## How RVN Fees Work

-   **Transaction size**
    
-   Paid in **RVN tokens**.
    

Edge estimates the fee automatically based on real-time network status.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High**
    
-   **Optimal (default)**
    
-   **Low**
    
-   **Custom**
    

---

## Average Cost

Typically **$0.005 to $0.02 USD** per transaction.

---

## Why It Matters

Ravencoin’s low fees are perfect for token creation and transfers. Edge makes sending RVN smooth and cost-efficient.
