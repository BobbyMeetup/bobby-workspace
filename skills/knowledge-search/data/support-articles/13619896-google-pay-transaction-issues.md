Quick help for Google Pay declines across Edge partners. Please find which partner is relevant for your transaction.

**Banxa**

**Why It Might Be Declined:**

1.  Unsupported card or region for Google Pay
    
2.  Incorrect authorization step
    
3.  Bank declined transaction
    

**What to Do:**

1.  Try a different card in Google Pay
    
2.  Use a different payment method
    
3.  Complete KYC
    
4.  Contact Banxa if still stuck
    
5.  [**Contact Banxa Support**](https://support.banxa.com/en/support/tickets/new)**: https://support.banxa.com/en/support/tickets/new**
    

**MoonPay**

**Why It Might Be Declined:**

1.  Insufficient funds or unsupported card
    
2.  Google Pay not verified with a 3D Secure card
    
3.  Incomplete MoonPay KYC
    
4.  Security filters or exceeded limits
    

**What to Do:**

1.  Use a 3D Secure-enabled card with Google Pay
    
2.  Complete verification on MoonPay
    
3.  Contact your bank
    
4.  Check MoonPay emails for alerts
    
5.  [**Contact MoonPay Support**](https://support.moonpay.com/)**: https://support.moonpay.com/**
    

**Paybis**

**Why It Might Be Declined:**

1.  Risk flagged by Paybis’ system
    
2.  Google Pay card not supported for crypto
    
3.  Bank rejected the payment
    

**What to Do:**

1.  Retry after 24 hours
    
2.  Try another Google Pay-linked card
    
3.  Complete KYC
    
4.  Check email from Paybis
    
5.  [**Contact Paybis Support**](https://support.paybis.com/)**: https://support.paybis.com/**
    

**Simplex**

**Why It Might Be Declined:**

1.  Insufficient funds or invalid card setup
    
2.  Google Pay not fully verified
    
3.  Bank or Simplex blocked the transaction
    
4.  Purchase limits exceeded
    

**What to Do:**

1.  Use a valid, verified card in Google Pay
    
2.  Finish verification on Simplex
    
3.  Contact your bank
    
4.  Check for emails from Simplex
    

[**Contact Simplex Support**](https://www.simplex.com/SUPPORT)**: https://www.simplex.com/SUPPORT**
