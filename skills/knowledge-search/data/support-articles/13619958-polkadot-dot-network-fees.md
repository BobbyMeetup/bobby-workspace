# Polkadot (DOT) Network – Scalable and Predictable

Polkadot is designed for interoperability and scalability, with a predictable fee structure to keep costs stable even during network congestion.

## How DOT Fees Work

Fees are based on:

-   **Transaction complexity**
    
-   **Network congestion (slight adjustment)**  
    Fees are paid in **DOT tokens**.
    

Edge dynamically calculates the right fee for your DOT transactions based on real-time network conditions.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – Fastest inclusion
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Smart balance
    
-   **Low** – Cheapest, slower confirmation
    
-   **Custom** – Set your own fee if preferred
    

---

## Average Cost

Sending DOT usually costs **$0.03 to $0.10 USD** depending on network activity.

---

## Why It Matters

DOT is built to remain affordable even during busy periods. Edge Wallet keeps it simple by suggesting the right fee based on speed or savings.
