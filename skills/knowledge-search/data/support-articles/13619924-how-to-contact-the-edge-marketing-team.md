If you have any marketing inquiries, reaching out to the Edge marketing team is simple. Follow these steps to ensure your message is directed to the right place:

# Step 1: Prepare Your Inquiry

Before contacting the team, clearly outline your marketing-related question or request. This could include advertising inquiries, promotional campaigns, or other marketing initiatives. Having a concise message will help the team respond efficiently.

# Step 2: Send an Email

The Edge marketing team can be reached via email. Compose your message and send it to the following address:

**Email**: [info@edge.app](mailto:info@edge.app)

Make sure to include a clear subject line, such as "Marketing Inquiry" or "Advertising Request," to help the team prioritize your request.

# Step 3: Await a Response

Once your email is sent, the Edge marketing team will review your inquiry and respond as soon as possible. Response times may vary depending on the volume of inquiries, so please allow a few business days for a reply.

# Important Note

If you have any marketing inquiries, please email **[info@edge.app](mailto:info@edge.app)**. This is the primary point of contact for all marketing-related matters.
