# Edge allows for acceleration of BTC using Replace-by-fee (RBF)

If you have a stuck BTC transaction you can now accelerate the transaction by adding a higher fee in a new broadcast to encourage miners to confirm your transaction.

To Accelerate or use the RBF function follow these steps:

1.  Enter Edge and then enter the Asset wallet with the stuck transaction.
    
2.  Tap on the pending transaction and then look for and top on the "Accelerate this transaction" option.
    
3.  Here you will see your old fee and the proposed new fee. If you agree with the new fee simply slide to confirm.
    

Your old pending transaction will be replaced with a new higher fee transaction that is more likely to confirm.
