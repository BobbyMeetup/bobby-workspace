Battery saver mode can hinder the Edge app's ability to function properly. Please disable any power-saving mode on your device before using the Edge cryptocurrency app.

**To disable Low Power Mode on your iPhone:**

-   Open Settings on your iPhone.
    
-   Scroll down and tap Battery.
    
-   Look for Low Power Mode. If it’s enabled, the toggle will be green.
    
-   Tap the toggle next to Low Power Mode to turn it off (it will switch to gray).
    

**To disable Battery Saver or Power Saving Mode on your Android device:**

-   Open Settings on your Android phone.
    
-   Scroll down and tap Battery (or Battery and Device Care on some devices).
    
-   Tap Battery Saver or Power Saving Mode. If it’s enabled, the toggle will be highlighted.
    
-   Tap the toggle to turn it off
