**Why Was My MoonPay Transaction Declined When Using Venmo?**

If your MoonPay transaction was declined while using Venmo, it could be due to issues with your Venmo balance, verification, or MoonPay’s payment processing filters. Here’s a quick overview of what might’ve gone wrong and how to fix it:

**Common Reasons**

1.  **Insufficient Funds:** Your Venmo balance or linked funding source doesn’t have enough funds
    
2.  **Verification Incomplete:** Venmo or MoonPay account may be missing required verification
    
3.  **Payment Method Issues:** Your Venmo-linked payment method (e.g. bank/card) may be unsupported or declined
    
4.  **Purchase Limits:** You may have exceeded Venmo’s or MoonPay’s transaction limits
    
5.  **Security Flag:** Transaction may have been auto-declined for risk or fraud prevention
    

**Recommended Actions**

1.  Confirm your Venmo balance and linked funding source have sufficient funds
    
2.  Make sure your Venmo and MoonPay accounts are fully verified
    
3.  Try using a different Venmo-linked payment method
    
4.  Check your email (and spam folder) for alerts from MoonPay or Venmo
    
5.  Retry the transaction after resolving any alerts or restrictions
    
6.  [**Contact MoonPay Support**](https://support.moonpay.com/)**:https://support.moonpay.com/**
