**Short Answer:** No, Edge is not a cold wallet. It is a **hot wallet**, meaning it is connected to the internet and designed for quick access to your crypto funds on mobile devices.

---

# What is a Cold Wallet?

A cold wallet refers to a crypto wallet that is **completely offline**, such as:

-   Hardware wallets (e.g., Ledger, Trezor)
    
-   Paper wallets (printed private keys)
    
-   Air-gapped devices (no network connection)
    

These are considered the most secure option for long-term storage since they are not vulnerable to online attacks.

# What is Edge Wallet?

Edge is a **non-custodial hot wallet** that prioritizes:

-   **User-controlled private keys**
    
-   **Client-side encryption**
    
-   **No third-party access to funds or data**
    

Your crypto keys are stored locally and encrypted on your device, not on a centralized server. Edge does not have access to your keys or assets.

# Security Features

While Edge is a hot wallet, it provides strong security through:

-   Auto-encrypted backups
    
-   Biometric and PIN protection
    
-   Support for 2FA on login
    
-   Open-source codebase for transparency
    

# Summary

If you're looking for a convenient mobile wallet that gives you full control of your assets with strong privacy and security—Edge is ideal.
