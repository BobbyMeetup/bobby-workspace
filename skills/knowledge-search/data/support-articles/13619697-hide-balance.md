**Showing or Hiding Your Balance**  
Users can toggle their balance visibility by tapping the balance or the eye icon.

This feature lets you conceal your balance in public or whenever you prefer to keep it private.

-   When the balance is **visible**, the eye icon displays a slash through it.
    
-   When the balance is **hidden**, the eye icon appears fully visible, and the balance is replaced with bold dots
