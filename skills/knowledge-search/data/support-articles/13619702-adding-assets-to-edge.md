# To add an asset to your Edge account , please follow these steps:

1.  Log into your Edge account and tap "View Assets" or use the Assets tab in the bottom navigation bar.
    
2.  Tap on the green "+" button to search for and select any asset(s) you want to add.  
    *Note: adding a token (i.e. PEPE, BONK) will also create its parent wallet (i.e. Ethereum, Solana)*
    
3.  Click 'Next' and rename these wallets (optional) for your account organization.
    
4.  Use the "Create Wallets" button, and you're done!  
    *The new wallet can be found on the Assets page in your account.*
    

Congrats on your wallet creation!
