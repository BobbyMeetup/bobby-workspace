Edge Wallet integrates with Bitrefill, allowing users to spend cryptocurrency on gift cards, prepaid mobile top-ups, and other digital services—directly from their wallet balance.

---

# Accepted Payment Methods

**Spend Crypto:**

Bitcoin (BTC)  
Ethereum (ETH)  
USDT (ERC20, TRC20, Lightning)  
Litecoin (LTC)  
Dash (DASH)  
Dogecoin (DOGE)

~0–1% network fee • Settlement: Instant to a few minutes depending on network

*\*Note: Bitrefill transactions are final. Ensure address and product details are correct before submitting payment.*

---

# Supported Regions

Bitrefill supports global users and offers gift cards and services for dozens of countries, with region-specific availability depending on the product.

**Supported Regions Include:**

North America: United States, Canada  
Europe: United Kingdom, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, and more  
Asia-Pacific: India, Philippines, Australia, and more  
Latin America: Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, Chile  
Africa: Nigeria, South Africa, Kenya, and others

**Restricted Regions:**

Bitrefill may restrict certain services based on regional compliance, product limitations, or payment availability.

*\*Please note that the list of supported regions is subject to change.*

---

# Getting Started

Open Edge Wallet: Launch the Edge Wallet app on your device.  
Navigate to Spend: Tap on the “Spend” or “Gift Cards” section.  
Select Bitrefill: Choose Bitrefill from the list of providers.  
Browse Products: Select a gift card or top-up service for your country.  
Pay with Crypto: Choose your payment currency and confirm the purchase.
