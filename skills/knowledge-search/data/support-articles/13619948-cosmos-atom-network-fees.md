# Cosmos (ATOM) Network – Hub of Blockchains

Cosmos is built for cross-chain communication, with quick and affordable transactions.

## How ATOM Fees Work

-   **Gas fees** based on network congestion
    
-   Paid in **ATOM**.
    

Edge checks network activity to recommend the best fee for your transaction.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – Prioritize confirmation
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Balanced
    
-   **Low** – Cheapest
    
-   **Custom** – Full gas manual setting
    

---

## Average Cost

Typically **$0.01 to $0.05 USD**.

---

## Why It Matters

Cosmos powers interoperability. With Edge, your ATOM transfers stay smooth and cheap, even during network peaks.
