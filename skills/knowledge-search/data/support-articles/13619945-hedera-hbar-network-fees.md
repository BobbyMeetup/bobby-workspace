# Hedera (HBAR) Network – Enterprise-Grade Efficiency

Hedera is a fast, energy-efficient network for both crypto and real-world applications.

## How HBAR Fees Work

-   **Flat fee model** based on transaction type
    
-   Paid in **HBAR**.
    

Edge handles the fee automatically without requiring user customization.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **Flat, predictable fee**.
    

---

## Average Cost

Generally around **$0.001 to $0.003 USD**.

---

## Why It Matters

HBAR is ultra-fast with flat low fees—ideal for microtransactions, NFT transfers, and enterprise needs. Edge Wallet makes sending HBAR simple and cheap.
