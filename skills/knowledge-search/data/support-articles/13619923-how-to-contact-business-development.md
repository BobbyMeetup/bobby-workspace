To contact the Edge Business Development Team regarding inquiries, please follow these steps:

-   Prepare a clear and concise summary of your business development inquiry or proposal, such as initiatives for growth or collaborative opportunities.
    
-   Send your correspondence to [info@edge.app](mailto:info@edge.app), including a subject line such as "Business Development Inquiry" to ensure prompt recognition.
    

The Edge Business Development Team will review your submission and respond within a few business days, depending on the volume of inquiries received.

For all business development matters, please direct your inquiries to [info@edge.app](mailto:info@edge.app), the primary contact point.
