All Edge accounts are encrypted on device before being backed up to the Edge servers. Bad actors will not be able to access accounts or funds even if the Edge servers are compromised. You can find more about this topic by reading our white paper: [Edge white paper.](https://support.edge.app/hc/en-us/articles/7019703449115-Edge-White-Paper)
