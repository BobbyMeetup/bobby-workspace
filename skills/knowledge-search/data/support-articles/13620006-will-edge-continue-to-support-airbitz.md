Edge has ceased support of Airbitz. Users will need to download Edge ([www.edge.app](http://www.edge.app)) and use their full username and password to gain access to their account. If you run into any issues or concerns please reach out to our support team at [support@edge.app](mailto:support@edge.app).

Note you will need your full username and password, pin will not work until after your first log in.
