Viewing your Seed Phrase / Master Private Key in Edge is very simple.

1.  First you will simply open the Edge app.
    
2.  Tap "Assets" located within the bottom navigation bar
    
3.  Viewing the list of wallets, find the wallet you want the Seed Phrase of, then tap on the wallet.
    
4.  Tap on the three vertical dots located at the upper right.
    
5.  Tap on 'Master Private Key' The following should appear:
    

Enter the password of your account and then the Seed Phrase / Master Private Key will appear.  
  
**NOTE:**

**DO NOT SHARE THE SEED PHRASE WITH ANYONE.**

**SHARING THIS INFORMATION WILL COMPROMISE THE SECURITY OF YOUR ACCOUNT AND YOUR FUNDS INSIDE THE WALLET.**  
  
**FUNDS INSIDE THE WALLET WILL BE IN DANGER OF BEING STOLEN BY ANYONE WHO VIEWS YOUR SEED PHRASE.**
