Getting Started:

1.  Open the Edge application.
    
2.  At the Login Screen, tap on **Get Started** to create a new account
    
    1.  Tap **Sign In** if you already have an account. Here you will be able to enter you username and password.
        
3.  Set up your PIN. Enter a 4 Digit PIN that you can remember.
    
4.  You are now in a Guest Account which will allow up to $50 of purchase and has some limited functions.
    

Unlocking all Edge features:

1.  Tap the "**Create Username & Password**" modal or notification at the bottom of the screen or visit the notification panel by tapping the menu located at the upper right. Then tap "Back Up Account".
    
2.  Enter a username of your choice. The username must be at least 3 characters and can include numbers and/or special characters.
    
3.  Set up your Password. The password must be at least 10 characters with 1 upper case letter and 1 number.
    
4.  Be sure to write down and store your password safely. Edge cannot reset your password nor do we have access to your account as we are completely zero knowledge.
    
5.  Account Confirmation - Your Username, Password, and PIN will be displayed once more. Write it down and accept the terms.
    

Confirm and Finish!
