Edge Wallet can automatically detect and hide spam or “dust” transactions so your transaction history stays clean and focused on the payments that matter. Here’s how to turn this feature on or off:

---

# What Are Spam Transactions?

Spam or “dust” transactions are tiny transfers—often sent by automated bots—that clutter your transaction list but carry negligible value. Hiding them helps you:

-   See only meaningful transfers
    
-   Avoid confusion over small, unwanted inputs
    
-   Keep your activity feed tidy
    

---

# Enabling (or Disabling) Spam‑Filter

1.  **Open Edge Wallet**  
    Launch the Edge app on your device and unlock it.
    
2.  **Go to Settings**  
    Tap the ☰ menu icon in the top‑right corner, then select **Settings**.
    
3.  **Navigate to Options**  
    Scroll down and find **Hide Spam Transactions**
    
4.  **Toggle Hide Spam Transactions**
    
    -   **On** = spam transactions are hidden.
        
    -   **Off** = all transactions, including dust, will be shown.
        
5.  **View Your Wallet**  
    Return to the **Assets** or **History** screen. Your spam transactions will now be filtered out (or restored, if you turned the feature off).
