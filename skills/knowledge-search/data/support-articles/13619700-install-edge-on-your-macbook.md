[Edge - Crypto & Bitcoin Wallet](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/edge-crypto-bitcoin-wallet/id1344400091) can also be downloaded from the Apple App Store onto your MacBook or iMac with any M-series chip if you want to run the app from your desktop. Edge is primarily focused on creating the best mobile experience, and all of our cutting-edge security protocols, such as biometric authentication and a robust [IP Validation and 2FA](https://support.edge.app/hc/en-us/articles/7018106439579) account system, will still function perfectly from your personal Mac.

*Operating Edge from a Chromebook or from a mobile phone emulator program on a Windows PC will also work; however, the app may not perform as quickly, and biometric sign-in will be unavailable.*

*WalletConnect is also available to you within Edge on your mobile device. This would allow you to interact with decentralized apps (dApps) on another device, such as a computer, while securely authorizing or signing transactions from your smartphone.*
