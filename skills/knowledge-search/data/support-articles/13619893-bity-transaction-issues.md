If your transaction with Bity was declined, it may be due to bank restrictions, transaction limits, verification issues, or internal security checks. Here's a quick overview of what might’ve gone wrong and how to fix it:

# Common Reasons

-   **Payment Issues**: Insufficient funds or incorrect bank transfer information
    
-   **Verification Incomplete**: ID, bank details, or documentation not fully verified
    
-   **Bank Restrictions**: Some banks block or delay crypto-related wire transfers
    
-   **Security Review**: Transactions may be auto-declined if flagged as suspicious
    
-   **Transaction Limits**: Exceeding Bity’s daily, monthly, or per-transaction limits
    

# Important Limit Notes

1.  **24-Hour Limit**: Bity has a €900 limit every 24 hours. This includes both completed and uncompleted orders. For example:
    
    -   If you complete an order for €400 and another for €500, you’ve hit the limit and must wait 24 hours from the latest order.
        
    -   If you start but don’t complete a €500 order, and then try to place a €600 order, you’ll be blocked because the system still counts the uncompleted €500.
        
2.  **Order Reset Needed**: If uncompleted orders are blocking you, contact Bity to reset your order history tied to your IBAN.
    
3.  **High Risk Country**: Bity may decline transactions from high-risk countries. To check your IBAN:
    
    -   Use this tool: https://resources.davidcoen.it/highriskchecker/
        
    -   It compares your IBAN's prefix against Bity’s country risk list here: https://data.bity.com/countries/compendium
        
    -   This check does not store your IBAN.
        

# Recommended Actions

-   Double-check your payment details and ensure funds are available
    
-   Complete all KYC/verification steps in your Bity account
    
-   Contact your bank to confirm they support crypto-related transactions
    
-   Monitor your email (and spam) for messages from Bity requesting additional info
    
-   Retry the transaction after verifying the steps above
