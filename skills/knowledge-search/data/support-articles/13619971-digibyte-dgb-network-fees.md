# DigiByte (DGB) Network – Fast and Ultra-Affordable

DigiByte is known for **fast block times** and **very low fees** — one of the quickest and cheapest UTXO blockchains.

## How DigiByte Fees Work

Fees depend on:

-   **Transaction size in bytes**
    
-   **Network congestion**
    

Edge uses public DigiByte nodes to suggest the best fee based on current conditions.

**DigiByte blocks are mined every ~15 seconds**, meaning confirmations are fast even with a low fee.

---

## Fee Options You Can Choose

-   **High** – Very fast confirmation
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Smooth balance
    
-   **Low** – Slowest but cheapest
    
-   **Custom** – Set your own fee manually
    

To adjust: Start a send → Tap **fee settings gear** → Pick or set a custom fee.

---

## Average Cost

A DGB transaction typically costs **less than $0.001 USD** — often fractions of a cent!

---

## Why It Matters

DigiByte is built for speed and affordability. Edge Wallet helps you pick the best fee with no technical headache—perfect for daily payments or quick moves.
