# zkSync Era – Speed and Scalability Together

zkSync Era is a next-generation Layer 2 that uses zero-knowledge rollups for fast, private, and cheap Ethereum transactions.

## How zkSync Era Fees Work

-   **Gas usage (gwei)**
    
-   **Network conditions**
    

Edge estimates zkSync fees using real-time network data.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – Fastest confirmation
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Best balance
    
-   **Low** – Slowest and cheapest
    
-   **Custom** – Set your own fee manually
    

---

## Average Cost

Roughly **$0.01 to $0.03 USD** per transaction.

---

## Why It Matters

zkSync Era gives you fast, cheaper transactions with added privacy. Edge makes fee management easy whether you need speed, privacy, or savings.
