*If you do not already have an Edge account, you may follow* [*this guide*](https://support.edge.app/hc/en-us/articles/7019038717339-How-do-I-create-an-account) *to create one.*

In summer of 2025, xStocks launched their tokenized equities on Solana. They have since launched on more blockchains, and Edge has already streamlined support for them on Arbitrum, BNB Chain, and Solana.

1.  Log into Edge and tap on "View Assets" or navigate to the Assets tab from the bottom navigation bar.
    
2.  Use the green + button to add a new wallet into your account.
    
3.  Search for 'xstock' and browse through the default list of tokenized stocks in Edge.
    
4.  Turn on any assets that you may want to trade.
    

*For any xStock asset that's not in the default list, you may use the 'Add Custom Token' button at the bottom of the list and manually add contract data for a token (advanced).*

In order to quickly and easily trade among these assets, you'll want to transfer or purchase the mainnet currency for the blockchain you'll be trading on -- SOL, BNB or ETH(Arbitrum). Here's an article on how [*Buying and Selling in Edge*](https://support.edge.app/hc/en-us/articles/7092223181467-Buying-and-Selling-in-Edge) works, so you may acquire any of these mainnet assets to get started.

Once you have a funded SOL into your Solana wallet - or - BNB into your Binance Smart Chain wallet - or - ETH into your Arbitrum wallet, you are ready to trade! Remember to always keep at least a small amount of the mainnet asset so you have funds for network fees required to broadcast transactions on-chain.

Lastly, access the in-app swap tool from the Exchange tab in the bottom nav bar, or by tapping the Trade button from within any of the wallets you have on your Assets page. More guidance on exchange can be found here: [How do I make an exchange?](https://support.edge.app/hc/en-us/articles/7097636138267-How-do-I-make-an-exchange)
