# Tezos (XTZ) Network – Smart Contracts with Low, Predictable Fees

Tezos offers smart contract functionality with self-amending governance and efficient transaction fees.

## How XTZ Fees Work

-   **Flat minimal fees**, paid in **XTZ**.
    
-   Fees may vary slightly depending on network load and smart contract complexity.
    

Edge dynamically estimates the best fee for your transaction.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – For quick inclusion
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Best balance
    
-   **Low** – Save a little more
    
-   **Custom** – Full manual fee setup
    

---

## Average Cost

Typically **$0.002 to $0.05 USD** per transaction.

---

## Why It Matters

Tezos provides efficient smart contracts and payments without crazy gas spikes. Edge makes sending XTZ smooth and easy with smart, automatic fee estimation.
