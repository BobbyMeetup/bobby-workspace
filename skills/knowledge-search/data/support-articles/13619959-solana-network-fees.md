# Solana Network – Blazing Speed at Tiny Costs

Solana is built for **high-performance applications** and is known for processing thousands of transactions per second at extremely low fees.

## How Solana Fees Work

Fees are determined by:

-   **Network usage**
    
-   **Transaction type (simple send, smart contract interaction, etc.)**  
    Fees are paid in **SOL tokens**.
    

Edge checks Solana’s real-time network conditions and suggests the right fee for your transaction.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – Prioritizes very fast execution
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Best everyday choice
    
-   **Low** – Cheapest but could be slightly delayed
    
-   **Custom** – Full manual control for advanced users
    

---

## Average Cost

A standard Solana transaction costs **less than $0.001 USD**—fractions of a cent!

---

## Why It Matters

Solana enables rapid and cheap blockchain interactions, ideal for DeFi, NFTs, and gaming. Edge Wallet simplifies Solana fee handling so you can stay fast and flexible without overcomplicating your send.
