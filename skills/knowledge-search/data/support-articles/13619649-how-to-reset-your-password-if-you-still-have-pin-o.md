Resetting your Edge account password is straightforward if you still have access via PIN or fingerprint. Edge’s security-first approach means only you control your credentials. This guide explains how to change your password when logged in through secure biometric or PIN methods, highlights alternative recovery options, and outlines next steps if recovery isn’t possible. Stay in control of your cryptocurrency and maintain your financial security with Edge.

# Resetting Your Password Using PIN or Fingerprint

1.  Log in to your Edge Wallet using your PIN or fingerprint authentication.
    
2.  Log out of your Edge account and log back in repeatedly (usually 4-5 times).
    
3.  After several login attempts, Edge will prompt you to set a new password.
    
4.  Follow the instructions to create a strong, unique password for your account.
    

# Using Recovery Questions and Recovery Token

-   If you’ve set up recovery questions, use them to verify your identity within the app.
    
-   Provide your Recovery Token if prompted to regain access to your Edge Wallet.
    

# If You Cannot Recover Your Password

Due to Edge’s zero-knowledge security, Edge cannot reset or access your password on your behalf. If you do not have your PIN, fingerprint, or recovery information, account access cannot be restored. Consider writing down or securely storing recovery options for future use.

# Conclusion

Maintaining control of your Edge credentials is essential for security and privacy. If you have PIN or fingerprint access, resetting your password is simple; if not, secure recovery options are your safety net. Remember that only you can manage your account, reinforcing Edge’s user-empowering, privacy-first ethos.
