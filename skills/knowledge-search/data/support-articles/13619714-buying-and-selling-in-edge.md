**B**uying and Selling Cryptocurrency in Edge

Edge makes it easy to buy and sell cryptocurrency directly within the app through our trusted partners. Here's how:

**Accessing Buy and Sell Options:**

-   Open the Edge app.
    
-   Locate the "Buy" and "Sell" buttons at the bottom of your screen. These are typically the two middle options.
    

**Selecting a Partner:**

-   Tap either "Buy" or "Sell."
    
-   You'll see a list of available options specific to your country or region. *Only the options displayed are available to you.*
    
-   Choose the option you wish to use for your transaction this will select a partner.
    

**Completing the Transaction:**

-   You'll be directed to the partner's platform to complete the transaction.
    
-   Follow the on-screen instructions and provide the necessary information.
    
-   **Buying:** After successful purchase, the partner will deposit the cryptocurrency into your selected Edge wallet.
    
-   **Selling:** After you send your cryptocurrency to the partner, they will deposit the equivalent fiat currency (e.g., USD, EUR) into your provided bank account.
    

**Important Notes:**

-   **Regional Availability:** The available buy and sell options are determined by your country or region.
    
-   **Edge's Role:** Edge facilitates access to these partners but does not handle the actual transactions.
    
-   **Data Privacy:** Edge does not store any private or sensitive customer data related to these transactions.
    
-   **Partner Support:** For any issues or questions regarding your purchase or sale, please contact the chosen partner directly.
