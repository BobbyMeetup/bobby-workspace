# PulseChain (PLS) – Fast and Cheap Ethereum Alternative

PulseChain is designed to lower costs compared to Ethereum.

## How PLS Fees Work

-   **Gas fees** measured in **PLS tokens**.
    
-   **Dynamic based on network congestion**.
    

Edge calculates the recommended gas fee automatically.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High**
    
-   **Optimal (default)**
    
-   **Low**
    
-   **Custom**
    

---

## Average Cost

Typically **$0.001 to $0.01 USD**.

---

## Why It Matters

PulseChain brings Ethereum apps cheaper to life. Edge makes sure your fees stay tiny and confirmations quick.
