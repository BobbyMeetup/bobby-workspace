# Tron (TRX) Network – The Sun Chain

Tron offers fast and cheap TRX transactions—but sending tokens like **USDT** (TRC20) can cost significantly more.

\*Fees listed are an estimation and can fluctuate greatly due to network activity and other factors.

## How Tron Fees Work

-   **TRX Transfers:** Use **bandwidth**, usually costing around **3 to 5 TRX**.
    
-   **Token Transfers (e.g., USDT):** Use **energy**, often costing **30 to 80 TRX**.
    
-   **New Wallet Activation:** Adds **1 to 5 TRX** extra if the receiving wallet is empty.
    

Edge Wallet automatically calculates fees based on your available bandwidth and energy.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **Flat cost** based on transaction type—no manual setting needed.
    
-   **Staking TRX** can earn free **bandwidth and energy** to lower or eliminate fees.

## How Staking TRX Helps

By staking TRX, you **generate free bandwidth and energy** every day.  
This can cover TRX sends and even lower the high energy costs for token transfers like USDT—saving you TRX over time.

---

## Why It Matters

Tron is one of the fastest and most affordable networks for basic TRX sends.  
For frequent USDT or token transactions, staking TRX is the smartest way to avoid high fees.  
Edge Wallet makes fee handling and tracking simple, whether you stake or pay as you go.
