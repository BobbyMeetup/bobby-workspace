**The Edge app does not offer any support through Telegram.**

Anyone claiming to be from Edge support and contacting you on Telegram is likely and imposter and should be reported and/or ignored.

We do have an official Telegram channel, here - [https://t.me/edgeappofficial](https://t.me/edgeappofficial) - which offers a stable link to our website, to download the app, and to our official support page. To combat scams and bad actors, *we do not send messages or allow others to send messages in this channel*.

Unfortunately, scammers often target users of cryptocurrency apps like Edge. They may impersonate customer support representatives or offer fake investment opportunities in an attempt to steal your cryptocurrency. It's important to be vigilant and never share sensitive information like passwords or private keys with anyone, even if they claim to be from Edge support.

If you need help with the Edge app, you may visit our help center ([support.edge.app](https://support.edge.app)) to learn more or submit a support ticket. You can also [email](mailto:support@edge.app) us, or call / text our official support line (+1.619.777.5688)

Remember, protecting yourself from scams is *crucial* when you self-custody your cryptocurrency. Always double-check the source of any communication and never share sensitive information with anyone.
