**Buying and Selling Cryptocurrency with Simplex in your Edge account**

Edge has partnered with Simplex to offer you a simple way to purchase cryptocurrency with a credit card. Purchased cryptocurrency is sent directly to your Edge wallet. Here’s how:

**How to Buy Crypto:**

1.  To see available options, tap the "Buy Crypto" or "Sell Crypto" section in your Edge account.
    
2.  Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your purchase.
    

**Available Cryptocurrencies:**

Simplex currently offers:

-   Bitcoin (BTC)
    
-   Bitcoin Cash (BCH)
    
-   Bitcoin SV (BSV)
    
-   Ethereum (ETH)
    
-   Litecoin (LTC)
    
-   USDT
    
-   (And other various cryptocurrencies, please check the buy crypto section of your edge wallet for a current list)
    

We are working to add support for more cryptocurrencies in the near future, so stay tuned!

**Fees:**

A total of up to 7% in fees are charged when buying cryptocurrency through Simplex in Edge:

-   Edge: 1% (no minimum)
    
-   Simplex: 5% ($10 minimum)
    

Note that your credit card company may charge additional fees, as they might treat the transaction as a cash advance. Please check with your credit card provider for any potential fees.
