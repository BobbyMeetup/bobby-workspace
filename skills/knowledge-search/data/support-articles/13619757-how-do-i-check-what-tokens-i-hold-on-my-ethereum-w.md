Edge is compatible with every token that utilizes the ERC20 standard. In order to offer this compatibility, the user has to enter the details specific to the tokens they want to view in their wallet. This means it’s possible for a token balance to exist on your ETH address but appear to be “missing”.

  
In order to view all of the spendable tokens associated to your ETH address, tap on your ETH address on the Receive screen to view it on an explorer and you'll see all the tokens associated to your address. To view these balances in your Edge wallet, add each token’s details according to the steps in our [blog post](https://blog.edgesecure.co/how-to-add-erc20-tokens-to-edge-4c4951c3a8cc).
