**Edge Wallet Home Screen Navigation Guide**

The Home Screen is your main control panel in the Edge Wallet app. Here's how to navigate it as a new user:

---

**Top Section: Total Balance & Quick Actions**

-   **Total Balance** Displays your combined crypto value in USD. Tap the eye icon to toggle visibility.
    
-   **View Assets** Tap to view all your wallets and token balances.
    
-   **Send** Tap to reveal three options:
    
    -   *To Bank /* Withdraw crypto funds to a fiat bank account.
        
    -   *To Edge*: Send crypto funds to Edge from Bank / Cash.
        
    -   *To Another Wallet / Exchange*: Transfer from Edge to an external wallet or exchange.
        
-   **Deposit** Tap to reveal two options:
    
    -   *From Bank / Cash*: Purchase crypto using traditional payment methods.
        
    -   *From Another Wallet / Exchange*: Receive crypto from another wallet or exchange.
        

---

**Main Features (Tiles Below Balance)**

-   **Buy Crypto**  
    Purchase cryptocurrencies using card, Venmo, PayPal, and other payment methods.
    
-   **Sell Crypto**  
    Convert your crypto back into fiat currency such as USD.
    
-   **Earn Crypto**  
    Stake supported cryptocurrencies to earn passive rewards.
    
-   **Swap Crypto**  
    Instantly trade one cryptocurrency for another within the app.
    

---

**Scroll Down for More**

-   **Markets**  
    View live price data of major cryptocurrencies.
    
-   **Edge-ucation**  
    Access helpful articles and videos designed to grow your crypto knowledge.
    
-   **Support**  
    Tap “Contact Support” to get direct help from Edge’s support team.
    

---

**Top-Right Menu (Three Lines)**

Tapping the three lines opens the main navigation menu, which includes:

-   **Current Account**: View the username currently logged in.
    
-   **Notifications**: See alerts related to app activity or updates.
    
-   **FIO Names / Requests**: Manage your human-readable crypto address.
    
-   **WalletConnect**: Connect your Edge Wallet to decentralized applications.
    
-   **Markets**: Quick access to market performance.
    
-   **Settings**: Customize your app preferences and security settings.
    
-   **Logout**: Safely log out of your Edge Wallet account.
    

---

**Top-Left Help Button (Help)**

At the top-left of the Home screen, you'll find additional support options including:

-   **Knowledge Base**: Access frequently asked questions and guides.
    
-   **Submit a Support Ticket**: Contact the support team for technical help.
    
-   **Call for Assistance**: Reach Edge support by phone.
    
-   **Visit the Edge Site**: Learn more about Edge on their official website.
    
-   **Terms of Service**: Review the platform’s legal and usage terms.
    

---

**Bottom Navigation Bar \*Swipe your finger down to see the full navigation bar and up to minimize it.**

-   **Home**: Return to the Home screen.
    
-   **Assets**: View all your wallets and token balances.
    
-   **Buy**: Access the buy crypto flow.
    
-   **Sell**: Access the sell crypto flow.
    
-   **Exchange**: Swap cryptocurrencies within the app.
