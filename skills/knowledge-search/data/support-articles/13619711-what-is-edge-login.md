Edge Login will serve as your zero knowledge Single Sign On to various blockchain applications. One Edge account, controlled by you and no one else, will give you private, secure, and easy access to the decentralized apps of today and those of tomorrow. This is the world’s first private and secure Single Sign On that does away with manual and error prone backup and handling of cryptographic keys, making you the center of your own unique multi-chain world. This will allow creators to create, technologists to innovate, and everyone to transact with peace of mind.
