# Zcash (ZEC) Network – Privacy with Efficiency

Zcash focuses on **privacy** while keeping fees low and predictable.

## How Zcash Fees Work

Like Bitcoin, Zcash fees are based on:

-   **Transaction size in bytes**
    
-   **Current network congestion**
    

Edge recommends fees after checking real-time Zcash network activity.

**Note:** Sending a private (shielded) ZEC transaction may require slightly more fees and time than a regular transparent one.

---

## Fee Options You Can Choose

-   **High** – Quick confirmation, useful for shielded transactions
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Balanced for most transfers
    
-   **Low** – Cheapest option if you're not in a rush
    
-   **Custom** – Set your own litoshis/byte manually
    

To adjust: Start a send → Tap **fee settings gear** → Choose a preset or enter custom values.

---

## Average Cost

Regular ZEC transactions cost around **$0.0001 to $0.005 USD** — extremely low!

---

## Why It Matters

Zcash lets you send privately or publicly at very low cost. Edge helps you adjust fees simply, whether you're prioritizing privacy, speed, or savings.
