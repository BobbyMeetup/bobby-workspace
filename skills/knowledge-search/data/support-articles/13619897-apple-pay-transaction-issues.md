Quick help for Apple Pay transaction issues across Edge's partners. Please find which partner is relevant to your transaction.

**Banxa**

**Why It Might Be Declined:**

1.  Unsupported region or card for Apple Pay
    
2.  Incorrect authorization entry
    
3.  Bank declined the transaction
    

**What to Do:**

1.  Try a different card
    
2.  Use another payment method
    
3.  Complete KYC
    
4.  Contact Banxa if still stuck
    
5.  [**Contact Banxa Support**](https://support.banxa.com/en/support/tickets/new)**: [https://support.banxa.com/en/support/tickets/new](https://support.banxa.com/en/support/tickets/new)**
    

**MoonPay**

**Why It Might Be Declined:**

1.  Insufficient funds or unsupported card
    
2.  Apple Pay card not 3D Secure
    
3.  Incomplete MoonPay verification
    
4.  Security filter or limit exceeded
    

**What to Do:**

1.  Use a 3D Secure-enabled card
    
2.  Complete MoonPay KYC
    
3.  Contact your bank
    
4.  Check for emails from MoonPay
    
5.  [**Contact MoonPay Support**](https://support.moonpay.com/)**: https://support.moonpay.com/**
    

**Paybis**

**Why It Might Be Declined:**

1.  Transaction flagged as risky
    
2.  Apple Pay card not accepted
    
3.  Bank blocked the payment
    

**What to Do:**

1.  Retry after 24 hours
    
2.  Try another card
    
3.  Complete KYC on Paybis
    
4.  Check for Paybis email alerts
    
5.  [**Contact Paybis Support**](https://support.paybis.com/)**: https://support.paybis.com/**
    

**Simplex**

**Why It Might Be Declined:**

1.  Invalid Apple Pay setup or card
    
2.  Verification incomplete
    
3.  Bank blocked the charge
    
4.  Exceeded Simplex limits
    

**What to Do:**

1.  Use a valid, verified card with Apple Pay
    
2.  Finish Simplex verification
    
3.  Contact your bank
    
4.  Look for messages from Simplex
    
5.  [**Contact Simplex Support**](https://www.simplex.com/SUPPORT)**: https://www.simplex.com/SUPPORT**
