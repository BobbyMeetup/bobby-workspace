# Arbitrum Network – Fast, Scalable Ethereum

Arbitrum brings cheaper, faster transactions to Ethereum by batching them off-chain and settling on Ethereum mainnet. Fees are paid in ETH, but they’re a fraction of the cost of L1 Ethereum.

## How Arbitrum Fees Work

Fees depend on:

-   **Gas usage (in gwei)** per transaction
    
-   **Network congestion**
    

Edge automatically calculates a smart gas price for you, using live Arbitrum network conditions.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – Fastest inclusion
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Smart balance of cost and speed
    
-   **Low** – Cheapest, slower confirmation
    
-   **Custom** – Full manual control
    

**To customize:** Start send → Tap **fee settings gear** → Choose your speed or set custom gas.

---

## Average Cost

Around **$0.02 to $0.10 USD** depending on congestion.

---

## Why It Matters

Arbitrum lets you use Ethereum dApps and assets for pennies instead of dollars. Edge makes fee management easy so you stay fast and cost-effective.
