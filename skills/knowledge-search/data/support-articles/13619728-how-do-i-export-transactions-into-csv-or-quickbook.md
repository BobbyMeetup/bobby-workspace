Edge can export your transaction history to use for tax purposes or personal records.

From the main wallet screen:

1.  Tap "Assets" located within the bottom navigation bar
    
2.  Tap on the wallet you'd like to export transactions from.
    
3.  Once inside the wallet tap on the three vertical dots located at upper right of the screen
    
4.  Tap "Export Transactions" and you'll have an option to export your wallet's transaction history as a QBP or CSV file. Select the date range that you'd like to export and then you can tap "Export" to send out to your preferred method.
    

NOTE: Edge does not send you tax documents such as 1099. Edge does not have access to user accounts.
