# Navigation Access: You can reach this screen at any time by tapping Exchange from the bottom navigation bar. \*Swipe your finger down to see the full navigation bar and up to minimize it.

**The Assets section provides a complete overview of your crypto holdings and wallets, with tools for managing and organizing them easily.**

# Top Section: Total Balance and Controls

-   **Total Balance**: Displays the combined USD value of all your crypto wallets.
    
-   **Eyeball Icon**: Tap to toggle visibility of your total balance for privacy.
    
-   **Send Button**: Opens three options:
    
    -   **To Bank / Cash** – Convert crypto to fiat for withdrawal.
        
    -   **To Edge** – Send crypto funds to Edge from Bank / Cash.
        
    -   **To Another Wallet / Exchange** – Transfer to external wallets or exchanges.
        
-   **Deposit Button**: Opens two options:
    
    -   **From Bank / Cash** – Buy crypto using fiat payment methods.
        
    -   **From Another Wallet / Exchange** – Receive crypto from outside sources.
        

# Wallet List

Each wallet row includes:

-   **Asset Logo & Name** (e.g., BTC, ETH, XRP)
    
-   **Current Price** and **Daily % Change**
    
-   **Wallet Balance** and **USD Equivalent**
    
-   **Wallet Nickname** (e.g., “My Bitcoin,” “My Ether”)
    

# Quick Access Buttons

-   **Green Plus (+)**: Tap to add a new wallet.
    
-   **Sort Icon (⇅)**: Adjust the wallet display order.
    

# Search Bar

-   Use the **Search Wallets** bar at the bottom to quickly find any wallet in your list.
    

# Scroll-Down Options

-   **Add Wallet**: Tap to create a new wallet for any supported crypto.
    
-   **Restore Wallets**: Retrieve previously archived or backed-up wallets.
