*\*Note: The Buy section varies by region. Available payment methods and providers depend on your country and state.*

****Navigation Access:** You can reach this screen at any time by tapping **Exchange** from the bottom navigation bar. **\*Swipe your finger down to see the full navigation bar and up to minimize it.****

The **Buy** section lets you purchase cryptocurrency using a variety of payment methods based on your location. This screen is designed to be quick, flexible, and beginner-friendly.

# Region Selection

-   At the top, is where you select your region country and state (e.g., United States of America – California).
    
-   You can tap this field to adjust your region if needed. The options shown below will update based on your selection.
    

# Payment Methods

Below the region selector, you’ll see a list of available payment methods with details for each:

-   **Venmo**
    
    -   **Fee**: ~5%
        
    -   **Settlement Time**: 5 minutes to 24 hours
        
-   **PayPal**
    
    -   **Fee**: ~5%
        
    -   **Settlement Time**: 5 minutes to 24 hours
        
-   **Apple Pay**
    
    -   **Fee**: 5–7%
        
    -   **Settlement Time**: 10 to 30 minutes
        
-   **Credit and Debit Card** (Visa, Mastercard)
    
    -   **Fee**: 5–7%
        
    -   **Settlement Time**: 10 to 30 minutes
        

-   **Revolut**
    
    -   Fee: Varies by provider
        
    -   Settlement: Usually within 30 minutes
        
-   **Bank Wire Transfer**
    
    -   Fee: Low (Sliding 2%)
        
    -   Settlement: 1–2 business days
        
-   **LibertyX (In-Person Cash)**
    
    -   Purchase crypto with cash at partnered retail locations
        
    -   Fees and availability depend on store and region
        

Each option can be tapped to begin the purchase process through a selected partner. You’ll be guided through entering the purchase amount, selecting a wallet to receive the crypto, and completing payment.
