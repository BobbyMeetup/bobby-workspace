**How to Buy with Bity:**

You can buy up to €900 per day with Bity by following the steps below.

1.  Open Edge and then tap on "Buy" to select the Bity or SEPA option. (NOTE: you will have to select your region)
    
2.  Select which asset you'd like to purchase and then enter the amount, press next.
    
3.  Here you will enter your bank information. Name, IBAN, and SWIFT/BIC
    
4.  Follow the additional confirmation and you have made a purchase.
    

You're all done! You'll receive the bitcoin in your Edge wallet in 1-2 business days.  
  
​

**How to Sell with Bity:**

You can sell up to €5000 per day with Bity by following the steps below.

1.  Tap "Sell" located at the bottom of the app and then tap the the Bity option. Noted by the "powered by Bity" at the lower right.
    
2.  Select the asset you wish to sell and then enter the amount.
    
3.  The next screen will require your bank information. Owner, IBAN and Swift/BIC.
    
4.  Once all information is entered simply review your order and "Slide to confirm"
    

You're all done! You'll receive the money in your bank account in 1-2 business days.

**What bank accounts are supported by BIty?**  
  
Bity supports SEPA bank transfers.
