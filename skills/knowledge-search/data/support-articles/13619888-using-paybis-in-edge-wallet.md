Edge Wallet integrates with Paybis, enabling users to purchase and sell cryptocurrencies seamlessly within the app.

# Accepted Payment Methods

**Buy Crypto:**

-   Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard) *Fee: ~5-7% • Settlement: ~10-30 Minutes*
    
-   Apple Pay (iOS devices) *Fee: ~5-7% • Settlement: ~10-30 Minutes*
    
-   Google Pay (Android devices) *Fee: ~5-7% • Settlement: ~10-30 Minutes*
    
-   PIX (Brazil) *Fee: ~5-7% • Settlement: ~5 MIN-24 Hours*
    
-   Revolut Pay (Available in select regions)*Fee: ~5-7% • Settlement: ~5 MIN-24 Hours*

# Supported Regions

Paybis's services are accessible in over 180 countries. However, certain regions are restricted due to regulatory reasons.

**Supported Regions Include:**

-   **North America:** United States (excluding New York and Louisiana), Canada
    
-   **Europe:** United Kingdom, Germany, France, Spain, Italy, Netherlands, and more
    
-   **Asia-Pacific:** Australia, New Zealand, South Korea, Japan, and others
    
-   **Latin America:** Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, Chile, and more
    
-   **Africa:** South Africa, Nigeria, Kenya, and others
    

**Restricted Regions:**

-   Afghanistan
    
-   Belarus
    
-   Cuba
    
-   Iran
    
-   North Korea
    
-   Syria
    
-   Venezuela
    
-   [Additional restricted regions](https://support.paybis.com/hc/en-us/articles/5118717092381-I-cannot-find-my-country-or-state-on-the-list-What-should-I-do)
    

*\*Please note that the list of supported regions is subject to change.*

# Getting Started

1.  **Open Edge Wallet:** Launch the Edge Wallet app on your device.
    
2.  **Navigate to Buy/Sell:** Tap on the "Buy" or "Sell" option.
    
3.  **Select Your Region:** Tap "Select Region" and choose your country/state.
    
4.  **Select Paybis:** Choose Paybis as your service provider.
    
5.  **Enter Details:** Input the required information and follow the prompts to complete your transaction.
