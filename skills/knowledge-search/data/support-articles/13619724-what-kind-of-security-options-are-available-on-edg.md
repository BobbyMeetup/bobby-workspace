-   **2 Factor Authentication (2FA)**
    
    -   Only your device can access the account even another device tries to access it with the known password
        
-   **Spending Limits**
    
    -   Requires a PIN/Password in order to send funds out
        
-   **Auto Logout**
    
    -   Automatically logs the account out after a set amount of time
        
-   **Fingerprint/Touch ID**
    
    -   Quick logins via fingerprint
        
-   **PIN Login**
    
    -   Quick logins via a 4 digit PIN
        
-   **Password Recovery**
    
    -   Set up recovery questions to recover your account and reset the password in case that is forgotten
