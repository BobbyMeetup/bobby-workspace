# Contact Edge

*If you still need assistance or have any questions about using the Edge app, please feel free to reach us through your preferred contact method below and we will be happy to help!*  
  
**Live Chat:**

-   You can live chat with us from any of the pages on our website and should see the icon in the below right of your screen now
    

**Text Message (SMS):**

-   Text us your questions, screenshots, and transaction information at any time and we will be happy to help review anything you need
    

**Phone Call:**

-   Setting us apart in the industry, you can speak 1-on-1 with our live support over the phone! Our agents are located around the world and all calls receive a callback within ~4-48 hours
    

**[Support Ticket](https://support.edge.app/hc/en-us/requests/new):**

-   Filing a support ticket helps give us all the information necessary to help resolve any issues or questions you may have - please include as much detail as possible
    

**Socials:**

-   Edge support is also available on *select* social media sites limited to the ones listed below
    

*Note: Edge does NOT offer support through WhatsApp or Telegram. Please avoid any imposters claiming to be Edge on those platforms (see* *common scams resources**)*

-   [Twitter](https://x.com/EdgeWallet)
    
-   [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/EdgeWallet)
    
-   [Instagram](https://twitter.com/EdgeWallet)
