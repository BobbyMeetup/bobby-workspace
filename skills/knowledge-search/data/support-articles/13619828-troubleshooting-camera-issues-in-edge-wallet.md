If you're having trouble launching your device camera while using Edge Wallet (such as during QR code scanning), follow these steps to resolve the issue.

# General Tips

-   Ensure your device's operating system is up to date.
    
-   Restart the Edge Wallet app and try again.
    
-   Restart your device.
    

---

# iOS Devices

## Step 1: Check Camera Permissions

1.  Open your iPhone **Settings**.
    
2.  Scroll down and tap **Privacy & Security**.
    
3.  Tap **Camera**.
    
4.  Find and toggle **Edge Wallet** to **ON** (green).
    

## Step 2: Check Screen Time Restrictions (if applicable)

1.  Go to **Settings**.
    
2.  Tap **Screen Time** > **Content & Privacy Restrictions**.
    
3.  Tap **Allowed Apps** and make sure **Camera** is allowed.
    

## Step 3: Reinstall Edge Wallet

1.  Delete the Edge Wallet app.
    
2.  Reinstall it from the **App Store**.
    
3.  Re-enable camera permissions when prompted.
    

---

# Android Devices

## Step 1: Check Camera Permissions

1.  Open **Settings**.
    
2.  Tap **Apps** or **Apps & Notifications**.
    
3.  Tap **Edge Wallet**.
    
4.  Tap **Permissions** > **Camera**.
    
5.  Select **Allow while using the app** or **Allow**.
    

## Step 2: Clear Cache (if issue persists)

1.  Go to **Settings** > **Apps** > **Edge Wallet**.
    
2.  Tap **Storage & cache**.
    
3.  Tap **Clear Cache**.
    

## Step 3: Reinstall Edge Wallet

1.  Uninstall the app.
    
2.  Reinstall from the **Google Play Store**.
    
3.  Re-enable camera permissions when prompted.
