# Zano (ZANO) Network – Secure and Private Smart Contracts

Zano is a privacy-focused network that supports both public and private transactions, with dynamic fees based on activity.

## How Zano Fees Work

Fees are based on:

-   **Transaction size**
    
-   **Private vs public transactions**
    
-   **Network conditions**  
    Fees are paid in **ZANO**.
    

Edge estimates the right fee for each transaction type automatically.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – For faster transactions
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Balanced
    
-   **Low** – Cost-saving option
    
-   **Custom** – Advanced manual fee setup
    

---

## Average Cost

Typically **$0.01 to $0.05 USD** for most transactions.

---

## Why It Matters

Zano lets you choose between open and private transactions at very low cost. Edge makes navigating fees simple so you can prioritize speed or savings easily.
