Edge Wallet integrates with Bits of Gold, enabling users in Israel to buy and sell cryptocurrency via bank transfers with local support and regulatory compliance.

---

# Accepted Payment Methods

**Buy Crypto:**

Bank Transfer (Israeli ILS accounts only)  
Fee: 5.9% • Settlement: 1–48 Hours

**Sell Crypto:**

Bank Transfer (Israeli ILS accounts only)  
Fee: 6.95% • Settlement: 1–48 Hours  
Level 1 Limit: €10,000 • Level 2 Limit: Unlimited

*\*Note: Bits of Gold services are only available in Israel. Payment method availability is subject to change and based on KYC verification.*

---

# Supported Regions

Bits of Gold is a licensed crypto brokerage operating within Israel and serving residents with Israeli bank accounts.

**Supported Region:**

-   Israel
    

**Restricted Regions:**

-   All regions outside of Israel
    

*\*Please note that the list of supported regions is subject to change.*

---

# Getting Started

Open Edge Wallet: Launch the Edge Wallet app on your device.  
Navigate to Buy/Sell: Tap on the "Buy" or "Sell" option.  
Select Your Region: Tap "Select Region" and choose Israel.  
Select Bits of Gold: Choose Bits of Gold as your service provider.  
Enter Details: Follow the instructions to complete your transaction via local bank transfer.
