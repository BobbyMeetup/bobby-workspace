**Why Was My Banxa Transaction Declined?**

If your Banxa transaction was declined, it’s likely due to payment issues, verification delays, or automated security checks.

**Common Reasons**

1.  **Payment Issues:** Incorrect card details or unsupported payment method
    
2.  **Verification Incomplete:** Missing or unverified ID/payment info
    
3.  **Bank Restrictions:** Some banks block or flag crypto-related transactions
    
4.  **Security Review:** High-risk transactions may be auto-declined
    
5.  **Order Timeout:** Delays in payment or verification can expire your order
    

**Recommended Actions**

1.  Double-check payment and ID details
    
2.  Use a different card or payment method
    
3.  Contact your bank to confirm that crypto transactions are permitted
    
4.  Look for Banxa emails (including spam) for verification steps
    
5.  [**Contact Banxa Support:**](https://support.banxa.com/en/support/tickets/new) [https://support.banxa.com/en/support/tickets/new](https://support.banxa.com/en/support/tickets/new)
