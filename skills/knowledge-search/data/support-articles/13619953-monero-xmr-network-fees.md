# Monero (XMR) Network – Private and Secure

Monero is the leading privacy coin, with every transaction shielded to protect sender, receiver, and amount details.

## How Monero Fees Work

Fees depend on:

-   **Transaction size**
    
-   **Privacy level**
    
-   **Network congestion**  
    Fees are paid in **XMR**.
    

Edge calculates fees based on Monero’s real-time blockchain status.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – Prioritize speed
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Balanced for most users
    
-   **Low** – Cheaper, slower
    
-   **Custom** – Manually adjust advanced settings
    

---

## Average Cost

Sending Monero usually costs **$0.02 to $0.20 USD**, depending on privacy complexity.

---

## Why It Matters

Monero’s privacy comes with slightly higher transaction data, but Edge Wallet simplifies fee choices while keeping your privacy intact.
