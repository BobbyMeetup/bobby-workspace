# Algorand (ALGO) Network – Instant and Predictable

Algorand is famous for fast, low-cost transactions with predictable flat fees.

## How ALGO Fees Work

-   **Flat minimum fee** (usually 0.001 ALGO)
    
-   **Small adjustments for very complex transactions**
    

Edge automatically applies the correct fee without needing user adjustment.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **One flat fee** — minimal and instant confirmation.
    

---

## Average Cost

Around **$0.0015 USD** per transaction.

---

## Why It Matters

Algorand’s reliability and cheap fees make it ideal for quick payments. With Edge, you get instant transactions without worrying about fee settings.

---

# Filecoin (FIL) Network Fees – Storage-Focused Transactions

Filecoin isn’t just crypto—it’s built for decentralized storage, but regular FIL transfers are still simple.

## How FIL Fees Work

-   **Base fee** based on network load
    
-   **Priority fee** if you want faster confirmation
    

Fees are automatically estimated by Edge.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – For quicker confirmation
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Standard for everyday transfers
    
-   **Low** – Accept slower confirmation to save
    
-   **Custom** – Full manual gas setting
    

---

## Average Cost

Typically **$0.05 to $0.20 USD** depending on network demand.

---

## Why It Matters

Filecoin manages real-world data, and Edge Wallet makes sending FIL fast and cost-effective with smart fee estimation.
