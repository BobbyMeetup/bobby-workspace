# Bitcoin Cash (BCH) Network – Fast, Low-Cost Transactions

Bitcoin Cash was created to offer faster transactions and cheaper fees than Bitcoin. It uses the same UTXO model, but with bigger block sizes, helping keep fees low even when the network is busy.

## How Bitcoin Cash Fees Work

Your BCH transaction fee depends on:

-   **The size of your transaction** (measured in bytes)
    
-   **Current network congestion**
    

Edge connects to public Bitcoin Cash nodes to estimate a smart fee in **satoshis per byte**.

**Example:** Larger transactions (more inputs/outputs) cost a little more, but BCH fees stay very low overall.

---

## Fee Options You Can Choose

-   **High** – Fastest confirmation, even during spikes
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Smart balance of speed and cost
    
-   **Low** – Slowest confirmation, cheapest fee
    
-   **Custom** – Manually set your sat/byte fee
    

To adjust: Start a send → Tap **fee settings gear** → Choose your speed or enter a custom rate.

---

## Average Cost

Sending BCH usually costs around **$0.002 to $0.01 USD**—almost nothing compared to Bitcoin!

---

## Why It Matters

Bitcoin Cash is perfect for fast, cheap everyday payments. Edge helps you pick the right fee automatically so you always get great value without overpaying.
