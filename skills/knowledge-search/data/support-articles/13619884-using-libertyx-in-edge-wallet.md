Edge Wallet integrates with LibertyX, allowing users in the U.S. to buy Bitcoin using cash or debit cards at thousands of local retail stores and ATMs.

---

# Accepted Payment Methods

**Buy Crypto:**

Cash (at participating stores and ATMs)  
~8% • Settlement: Instant to 1 Hour

Debit Card (at eligible ATM locations)  
~8% • Settlement: Instant to 1 Hour

*\*Note: Payment method availability is subject to change and also varies by location.*

---

# Supported Regions

LibertyX is available exclusively in the United States and operates through a nationwide network of partner retailers and ATMs.

**Supported Regions Include:**

United States (select locations only)

-   Includes major retail chains like CVS, Rite Aid, and 7-Eleven
    
-   ATM coverage available in most states
    

**Restricted Regions:**

LibertyX is **not available outside the U.S.** and may not operate in all U.S. states or rural locations.

*\*Please note that the list of supported regions is subject to change.*

---

# Getting Started

Open Edge Wallet: Launch the Edge Wallet app on your device.  
Navigate to Buy/Sell: Tap on the "Buy" option.  
Select Your Region: Tap "Select Region" and choose your state.  
Select LibertyX: Choose LibertyX as your service provider.  
Follow Instructions: Use the app to find nearby locations, generate a purchase code, and complete your transaction at a participating store or ATM.
