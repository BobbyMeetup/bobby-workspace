The Edge support team can be reached by visiting our website [https://edge.app/support/](https://edge.app/support/) which will give access to our articles, support tickets, and chat.

You can also open Edge and tap Help for options to reach our support team such as submit a ticket, call, etc.

If for any reason you do not have access to the app you are always welcome to contact

By sending an email to [support@edge.app](mailto:support@edge.app), or by calling 1-619-777-5688

For partnership and business inquiries please reach out to [marketing@edge.app](mailto:marketing@edge.app).
