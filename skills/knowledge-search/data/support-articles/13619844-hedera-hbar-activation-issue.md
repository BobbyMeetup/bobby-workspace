**Hedera (HBAR) Wallet Activation Update**  
The activation service for Hedera (HBAR) is no longer operational. As a result, new HBAR wallet addresses require a small deposit—0.01 HBAR or less—to become active. You can send this amount from any HBAR wallet with a balance that supports the address format.

Note that addresses generated at wallet creation may not be compatible with certain exchanges, such as Coinbase or Kraken. These platforms might require a different address type or a memo for transactions, so please verify their specific requirements before sending funds.
