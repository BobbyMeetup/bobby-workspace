# Ethereum Classic (ETC) Network – Classic Chain, Simple Fees

Ethereum Classic is the original Ethereum chain, offering smart contract support with lower traffic and cheaper fees compared to Ethereum.

## How ETC Fees Work

-   **Gas price (gwei) x Gas limit** (same model as Ethereum)
    
-   Paid in **ETC** tokens.
    

Edge automatically estimates fees based on current ETC network congestion.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – Fastest confirmation
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Balanced cost and speed
    
-   **Low** – Cheapest option
    
-   **Custom** – Manually set gas price and limit
    

---

## Average Cost

Typically **$0.005 to $0.20 USD** per transaction, depending on network traffic.

---

## Why It Matters

ETC allows you to use smart contracts with Ethereum tech, but at much lower costs. Edge Wallet simplifies fee picking to keep your sends fast and affordable.
