# USDC vs. wrapped ETH USD.e on Polygon: A Tale of Two Stablecoins

**Polygon has two versions of USDC:**

**USDC, native to Polygon,** boasts direct issuance by Circle, a trusted custodian. Backed by real-world reserves, it offers stability and seamless integration with Circle's financial infrastructure.

**Wrapped ETH USD.e,** conversely, hails from Ethereum via bridges. It represents Ethereum wrapped in a Polygon-compatible cloak, enabling DeFi users to leverage Ethereum's liquidity on Polygon's faster, cheaper tracks.

While both options provide excellent stablecoin alternatives on the Polygon network, **double-check the contract address before sending**. Mistakes can lead to irreversible losses.
