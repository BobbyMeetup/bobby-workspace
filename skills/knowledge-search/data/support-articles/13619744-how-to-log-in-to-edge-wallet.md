Edge Wallet provides a secure and user-friendly way to manage your crypto. Here’s how to log in to your Edge account on your mobile device.

# Step-by-Step Instructions

## Step 1: Open the App

-   Tap to launch the **Edge Wallet** app on your iOS or Android device
    

## Step 2: Enter Username

-   Tap Sign In (found below the get started button)
    
-   Tap the username field and enter your **Edge username**
    

## Step 3: Enter Password

-   Enter your secure **password** associated with your account
    

## Step 4: Optional – Enable Biometric Login

-   If you’ve enabled Face ID, Touch ID, or a fingerprint sensor, you may use it instead of entering your password manually
    
-   To access your PIN or Biometric ID tap on the green arrow found next to the username field. Then tap on the username you would like to use your pin or biometric ID for.
    

## Step 5: Log In

-   Tap **Log In** to access your wallet
    

---

# Trouble Logging In?

If you’re having issues:

-   Make sure your **internet connection** is stable.
    
-   Double-check your **username and password**.
    
-   Tap **Forgot Password** to use your recovery token.
