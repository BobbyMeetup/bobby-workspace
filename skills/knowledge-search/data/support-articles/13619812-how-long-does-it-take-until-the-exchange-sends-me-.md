This typically takes anywhere from a few minutes up to a few hours to complete.

The factors that could lead it to taking longer than usual could be due to:

-   The traffic on the network and the time it takes for the initial transaction to confirm
    
-   Exchange liquidity of the coin
    

If you experience an exchange transaction that is taking longer than 24 hours to complete, we recommend reaching out directly the Exchange partner for support or contacting our support team at [support@edge.app](mailto:support@edge.app).
