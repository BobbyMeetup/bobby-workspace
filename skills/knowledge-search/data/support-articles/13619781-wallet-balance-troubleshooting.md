Each wallet in your Edge account will display its balance in its native value and in your choice of fiat currency when you first created the wallet. To view a particular wallet simply tap on it when viewing the list of wallets in the app. All the transaction data and wallet balance will display there.

  
If your Edge app seems to not be accurate or you believe you're having synchronization issues, you can view your wallet data publicly as well.  
  
You will need to grab your wallet address with the follow steps:  
  
1\. Tap "Assets" located within the bottom navigation bar

2\. Tap on the wallet of choice

3\. Tap 'Receive'

4\. Tap 'Copy' at the bottom of the screen

This will put the address on your clipboard so you can use it later  
  
Then paste that address into the particular block explorer to view your data. Below are some of the more popular options available. If your wallet's block explorer is not provided below, they can often be found by typing "\[chain\] explorer" chain being whatever type of blockchain you're working with.  
  
i.e. "Monero Explorer", "Stellar Explorer" etc.

[https://blockchair.com/](https://blockchair.com/)  
  
Ethereum  
[https://etherscan.io/](https://etherscan.io/)  
  
Polygon / MATIC  
[https://polygonscan.com/](https://polygonscan.com/)
