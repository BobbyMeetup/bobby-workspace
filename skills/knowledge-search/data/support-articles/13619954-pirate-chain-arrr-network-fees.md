# Pirate Chain (ARRR) Network – Privacy First

Pirate Chain is a privacy-focused blockchain where all transactions are shielded for full anonymity.

## How ARRR Fees Work

ARRR uses:

-   **Transaction size in bytes**
    
-   **Network congestion**  
    Fees are paid in **ARRR tokens**.
    

Edge estimates the right fee dynamically based on transaction size and current network activity.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – Faster confirmation
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Best for everyday use
    
-   **Low** – Cheapest option
    
-   **Custom** – Set your own if needed
    

---

## Average Cost

ARRR transactions typically cost **$0.01 to $0.10 USD** depending on complexity.

---

## Why It Matters

Privacy can require a bit more data and slightly higher fees, but ARRR keeps it affordable. Edge lets you adjust fees easily without compromising privacy.
