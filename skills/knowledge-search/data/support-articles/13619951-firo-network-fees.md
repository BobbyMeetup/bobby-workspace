# Firo (FIRO) Network – Private and Accessible

Firo (formerly Zcoin) focuses on financial privacy through Lelantus technology and efficient transactions.

## How Firo Fees Work

Fees are based on:

-   **Transaction size**
    
-   **Privacy features used**  
    Paid in **FIRO** tokens.
    

Edge estimates fees automatically depending on whether you’re sending private or regular transactions.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – For faster confirmations
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Balanced approach
    
-   **Low** – Save on fees, slower confirmation
    
-   **Custom** – Advanced manual settings
    

---

## Average Cost

Typically **$0.01 to $0.10 USD** per transaction.

---

## Why It Matters

Firo balances privacy and cost effectively. Edge Wallet helps you choose your fee simply while protecting your anonymity.
