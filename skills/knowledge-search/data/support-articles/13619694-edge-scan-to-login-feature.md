Need to access your Edge account on a new device? Our "Scan to Login" feature offers a secure and speedy way to do just that by using a QR code. Here's how:

**On the New Device (where you want to log in):**

1.  **Get Edge:** Download and install the official Edge app from [www.edge.app](https://www.edge.app/) or from your app store.
    
2.  **Open the App:** Launch Edge on your new device.
    
3.  **Tap "Already have an account? Sign in":** You'll see this option on the first screen near the bottom.
    
4.  **Find the QR Code:** On the next screen, below the "Create account" button, you'll see a QR code. Keep this screen open.
    

**On Your Logged-In Device (the one you're currently using):**

1.  **Open Edge:** Make sure you're logged into your Edge account on this device.
    
2.  **Access the Menu:** Tap the three horizontal lines (menu icon) located at the top right corner.
    
3.  **Select "Scan QR":** Choose this option from the menu. Your camera will activate.
    
4.  **Scan the Code:** Point your camera at the QR code displayed on the new device. Ensure it's clearly within the frame.
    
5.  **Confirm Login:** Your current device will ask you to confirm the login to the new device. Tap "Confirm".
    

Your Edge account is now active on your new device.

**A Word on Security:**

This feature provides convenient access, so please be mindful:

-   Only scan QR codes displayed on devices you intend to log in to.
    
-   Be cautious of scanning codes from unfamiliar or untrusted sources.
    
-   Always keep your primary, logged-in device secure.
