1.  Log into your Edge account.
    
2.  Tap on the Send button
    
3.  Pick the best option. If you're sending to another wallet select the bottom option
    
4.  Select the asset you would like to send
    
5.  You can scan the QR Code to send to or tap on the Address button and paste the text address you would like to send to
    
6.  Once the address is scanned or entered, you will be able to enter the amount that you wish to send
    
7.  Slide to confirm when you are ready to send the amount entered
