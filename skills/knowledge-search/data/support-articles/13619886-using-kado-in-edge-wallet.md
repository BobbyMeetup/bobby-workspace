Edge Wallet integrates with Kado, enabling users to **buy and sell cryptocurrencies** using direct bank-based payments.

# Accepted Payment Methods

**Buy Crypto (via Edge Wallet):**

-   **Bank Wire Transfer (United States only)** *Fee: ~2% • Settlement: ~5 Minutes • Limit: $10,000*
    
-   *KADO Bank Wire Transfer Address 100 Summer St, Ste 1600, Boston, MA 02110*
    

**Sell Crypto:**

-   **ACH Bank Transfer (United States only)** *Fee: ~1.5% • Settlement: 2–3 Business Days*
    

*\*Note: Payment method availability varies by country and region.*

---

# Supported Regions

Kado’s services are available in over 150 countries. However, service availability depends on regional regulations and compliance.

**Supported Regions Include:**

North America: United States, Canada  
Europe: United Kingdom, Germany, France, Spain, Italy, Netherlands, and more  
Asia-Pacific: Australia, New Zealand, South Korea, Japan, and others  
Latin America: Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, Chile, and more  
Africa: South Africa, Nigeria, Kenya, and others

**Restricted Regions:**

-   Afghanistan
    
-   Belarus
    
-   Cuba
    
-   Iran
    
-   North Korea
    
-   Syria
    
-   Venezuela
    

*\*Please note that the list of supported regions is subject to change.*

# Getting Started

1.  **Open Edge Wallet:** Launch the Edge Wallet app on your device.
    
2.  **Navigate to Buy/Sell:** Tap on the "Buy" or "Sell" option.
    
3.  **Select Your Region:** Tap "Select Region" and choose your country/state.
    
4.  **Select Kado:** Choose Kado as your service provider.
    
5.  **Enter Details:** Follow the prompts to complete your transaction.
