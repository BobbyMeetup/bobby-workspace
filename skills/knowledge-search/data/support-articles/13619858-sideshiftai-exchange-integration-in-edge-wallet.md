**Type:** Centralized Exchange (CEX)

Centralized exchanges must follow anti-money laundering (AML) laws. In order to stay within compliance, they may have trading limits and may also require your identity to meet Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements.

**Overview:** Sideshift.ai is an instant cryptocurrency exchange that supports crypto swaps without registration under most conditions. Sideshift.ai may request verification if a transaction is flagged for risk or suspected to be linked to illicit activity. Flagged transactions are reviewed by a human and may require identity verification before a refund is issued. The service collaborates with third-party risk detection tools and law enforcement to ensure regulatory compliance.

**Email Support:** Users can send an email to help@sideshift.ai to receive support.

# How to Manage Exchange Settings in Edge

To access your exchange settings in Edge Wallet:

1.  Tap the **three lines** in the upper right-hand corner
    
2.  Tap on **Settings**
    
3.  Tap **Exchange Settings**
    
4.  Here, you can turn exchange partners **on or off**, and customize your preferences. When multiple exchanges can fill an order, you can also choose to automatically select the best price or prioritize a preferred exchange.
