Force closing is a method of shutting down the app running on your device. Please note this is not uninstalling. Uninstalling should be avoided if we are troubleshooting issues.

**Force close on Android:**

**Method 1:**

-   Swipe down from the top of your device to show the notification panel
    
-   Swipe down again and then tap on the "Gear" or settings icon
    
-   Tap on "Apps"
    
-   Find and tap on Edge
    
-   Tap "Force Stop"
    

**Method 2:**

-   From the Home Screen, swipe up from the bottom of the screen. Swipe slow to see all open apps. NOTE: On Samsung the same action is the 3 vertical lines or on Android devices without gesture the square icon
    
-   Swipe right or left to locate Edge
    
-   Swipe up on the app preview to close Edge
    

**Force close on iOS:**

-   From the Home Screen, swipe up from the bottom of the screen. Swipe slow to see all open apps
    
-   Swipe right or left to locate Edge
    
-   Swipe up on the app preview to close Edge
