[https://electrumsv.io/download.html](https://electrumsv.io/download.html)

2\. Open up the app and click ‘Continue’

5\. Upon entering your newly created wallet, an Account must be added by clicking the ‘Add Account’ button in the top-left corner.  
  
​

![](https://edge-1a5ed69e1271.intercom-attachments-7.com/i/o/ourx4xix/2027068258/5854ae3abbda6fe225b8478d9f44/NzSLkHgIP2Bgnuu3eoujT5y88dnX95_CLjNpLaVWxbh9PJmNMigfbBUYpvbk2uZX_WchCXOy3ppxNdvN-Nu599qyM6sC9MFrdvOd9-u14NYykUiEN-thpKEp0wFhpnXT36jJwafnBj9dwJ3VMVmCR5o?expires=1770786000&signature=4b704b4d3778d98dbd8deb6c0f96000ae4084d4f9c245d9147dbd925dfd8b2a1&req=diAlEcl4lYNaUfMW1HO4zTdwBXJYnSpMd9FU%2F6KWnKDLWgq1sfl8f9qdbhXr%0AsvjL%0A)

6\. Here, we must select the option to ‘Import from text (any seed phrase …)’ and press ‘Continue’  
  
​

![](https://edge-1a5ed69e1271.intercom-attachments-7.com/i/o/ourx4xix/2027068315/28d22660ab7e8ad89926b0d891f9/qduCnX6Yxv1YcJ98FR69r3calq3gAqtztWqx3WSi8Jdahg5xr40rgmbkgFk6KzPRSWyhEXE3-CEU9mjHbGCgmyQtwRKKpb7GVPlodjWB6AcabcNd2CEiJPTxPIcsnGlIa5h0bBI_-jpXRojKYNDHe30?expires=1770786000&signature=3a9381190653d4ab857f8c0e66999989fed7b3ed395b4374d6670f7b89095cc9&req=diAlEcl4lYJeXPMW1HO4zVrKDE2TuHdQF2wPaCsWap3rhHyhYym3tCloqwfH%0ACZ2H%0A)

7\. Enter in your 24-word private seed phrase from Edge  
  
​

*To get your BSV seed phrase out of Edge: click into the BSV wallet, tap the three vertical dots, and select ‘Get Raw Keys’ to view your 24-word “bitcoinsvKey” - Also take note of the “coinType” number at the top of this. BSV is 236, but it could be different if the wallet originally was for BTC, BCH, etc.*

NOTE: Please be cautious with your seed phrase. Anyone with access to this seed phrase will have equal access to your funds.

**Important: After entering your 24 words, click on ‘Customize’** (do NOT click ‘Done’)  
  
​

8\. Modify the Coin Type (the first 0’), to 236, or whichever “coinType” number you found in the Raw Keys file for the BSV wallet you’re holding in Edge.
