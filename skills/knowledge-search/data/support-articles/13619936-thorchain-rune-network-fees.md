# Thorchain (RUNE) Network – Cross-Chain Swaps Made Simple

Thorchain allows users to swap assets across blockchains without centralized exchanges, with fees based on swap complexity and traffic.

## How RUNE Fees Work

-   **Transaction size and network congestion**.
    
-   Swaps involve two transactions (inbound + outbound), each paid separately in **RUNE** or the swapped asset.
    

Edge estimates and displays the required fees clearly before you confirm.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **Swap fees calculated dynamically** — no manual adjustment needed.
    

---

## Average Cost

Varies depending on the swap, but generally **$0.50 to $3.00 USD** total per swap pair (both sides).

---

## Why It Matters

Thorchain unlocks true decentralized trading across chains. Edge makes sure your swap fees are fully transparent and optimized without needing to dive into complicated settings.
