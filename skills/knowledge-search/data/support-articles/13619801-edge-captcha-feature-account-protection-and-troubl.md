Edge uses Captcha to protect accounts against spam. The Captcha appears as a checkbox or image selection task.

**Troubleshooting Captcha Issues:**  
Users may face challenges with the Captcha. If any of these occur, [force close](https://support.edge.app/hc/en-us/articles/26702768694811) the Edge app and attempt login again:

-   Captcha does not load
    
-   Images are invisible or partially displayed
    
-   Verification fails despite correct responses or multiple tries
    

**Force Close Steps:**

[How to force close Edge (Android and iOS).](https://support.edge.app/hc/en-us/articles/26702768694811)

**Further Assistance:**  
If issues persist, ensure a stable internet connection or update the app.
