To change the displayed fiat currency (your local money) in Edge, please follow these steps:

1.  Log in to your Edge account
    
2.  Tap the three horizontal lines (≡) in top-right of screen to open the side menu
    
3.  Tap on the 'Settings' near the bottom of this menu
    
4.  Scroll down, under the 'Options' section, tap on "Default Currency"
    
5.  Search for or scroll and select your preferred fiat currency
    

**Note:** This setting can be changed anytime, and applies globally, affecting your 'Total Balance' and individual fiat values seen for each asset in your account.
