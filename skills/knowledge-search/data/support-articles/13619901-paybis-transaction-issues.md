**Why Was My Paybis Transaction Declined?**

If your cryptocurrency transaction on Paybis has been declined, it may be due to security measures designed to protect users and the platform.

**Common Reasons**

1.  **Risk Concerns:** Transactions flagged as risky may be blocked — specifics are not disclosed
    
2.  **Bank Restrictions:** Some banks block crypto or treat it as a cash advance
    
3.  **Unauthorized Payments:** Report any unrecognized charges to Paybis support
    

**Recommended Actions**

1.  Retry after 24 hours
    
2.  Use an alternative payment method
    
3.  Contact your bank about crypto-related restrictions
    
4.  Check your email (including spam) for Paybis verification requests
    
5.  [**Contact Paybis Support**](https://paybis.com/contacts/)**: https://paybis.com/contacts/**
