**Airbitz is no longer functional. - [Will Edge continue to support Airbitz?](https://support.edge.app/hc/en-us/articles/7072599431963)**

Airbitz uses older code and may have issues detecting transactions. Airbitz was deprecated in 2019. **We recommend users to upgrade and install the Edge Wallet which is our newest and most up to date product. Head over to [https://edge.app/](https://edge.app/) or search for "Edge Wallet" on the Playstore or App Store.**

**Once installed, simply sign in with the same username and password that you use for Airbitz**. The transaction should be detected and the account properly synced almost immediately.

Edge is our newest product and does a better job in regards to detecting transactions and account syncing. Additionally, it has a lot more new features such as being able to support other cryptocurrencies and being able to swap between other coins and tokens.
