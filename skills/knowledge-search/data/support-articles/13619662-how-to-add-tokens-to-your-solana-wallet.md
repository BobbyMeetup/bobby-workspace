To add a token to your your Solana (SOL) wallet simply navigate to your assets by tapping the assets tab in the bottom navigation and then tap on your SOL wallet.

Tap the three vertical dots and then tap an Add/Edit tokens to view the available default tokens.

Additionally you can add custom tokens:

Tap on the SOL wallet you want to add a token to and then tap on the three vertical dots located at the upper right of the wallet screen. You will be presented with a menu that will have "Add/Edit Tokens" which once tapped functions as the Add Token option at the bottom of the screen.

This will bring you to the Manage Tokens screen where you can tap on "Add Custom."

-   **On this screen you will need to enter the following:**
    
    -   **Token Name**
        
    -   **Token Symbol**
        
    -   **Contract Address**
        
    -   **Decimal Places**
        

This information can be found on [Solana Explorer](https://explorer.solana.com/) by typing in the token's code. All of the information will be viewable under the "Overview" section.

-   Once added make sure that it is checked in the Manage Token Screen
    
-   You will now see the newly added Token at the Wallets List Screen
    

You will be able to store, receive, and send that token out as you wish.

Note that you will need some of the parent coin to send out any SOL token.

Tokens can not pay their own network fees.
