# How to Use Edge on Multiple Devices:

There are two convenient ways to access your existing Edge account on a new device:

**1\.** **Using Your Username and Password:**

This is the traditional method and works seamlessly across all platforms.

-   **On the New Device:**
    
    1.  **Install Edge:** Download and install the official Edge application from your device's app store (available on [www.edge.app](https://www.edge.app/) .
        
    2.  **Open Edge:** Launch the Edge app.
        
    3.  **Tap "Already have an account? Sign in":** This option is located at the bottom of the screen on the initial app opening. Tap it.
        
    4.  **Enter Your Credentials:** Carefully enter the **exact same username and password** you use for your existing Edge account.
        
    5.  **Tap "Sign In"** (or the equivalent button).
        
    6.  Approve the login from your logged in device. You will receive a notification that a device is attempting to access your account. Tap to approve.
        
    
    You're all set! Your Edge account will now be accessible on this new device.
    

**2\.** **Using the Scan to Login Feature (For a quick and easy connection from a logged-in device):**

If you already have Edge open and logged in on another device, this method offers a super-fast way to connect.

-   **Learn More:** For detailed steps on using this incredibly convenient feature, please refer to our dedicated article: [Edge Scan to Login Feature](https://support.edge.app/hc/en-us/articles/36235155784475)
    

**Important Note:**

-   As long as you use the same username and password, all your devices will be linked to the same Edge account
    
-   Need to create an account for the first time? Click here to learn how: [How do I create an account?](https://support.edge.app/hc/en-us/articles/7019038717339)
    

With Edge, managing your digital assets across multiple devices has never been easier. Enjoy the freedom and flexibility!

**Security notice: Only approve login requests on your logged-in device if you initiated them and recognize the new device.**
