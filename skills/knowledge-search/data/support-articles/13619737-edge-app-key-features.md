**Core Functionality:**

-   **Multi-Asset Support:**
    
    -   Supports a wide range of cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin (with Segwit), Litecoin (with Segwit), Bitcoin Cash, Ethereum, Monero, Ripple (XRP), Binance Chain, Feathercoin, Dash, and all ERC20 tokens.
        
-   **Built-in Exchange:**
    
    -   Allows users to seamlessly exchange between different digital currencies and tokens, leveraging the best available rates from multiple partner exchanges.
        
-   **Buy and Sell Crypto:**
    
    -   Enables users to purchase and sell cryptocurrencies directly using their bank accounts, cash, debit or credit cards.
        
    -   Different options are available globally, please enter the buy or sell section of the Edge app to check what is available in your region.
        

**Security and Privacy:**

-   **Zero-Knowledge and Private:**
    
    -   Edge employs a zero-knowledge architecture, meaning Edge itself cannot access or view user accounts.
        
    -   Accounts are not linked to any personal identity, enhancing user privacy.
        
    -   Users maintain complete control over their funds, accessible 24/7, even in the event of Edge's discontinuation.
        
-   **Decentralized Control:**
    
    -   Edge is designed for global accessibility.
        
    -   Users have 100% full control of their funds, with no third-party access.
        

**Key takeaways for the customer:**

-   Edge offers a wide variety of supported crypto assets.
    
-   It is very focused on user security and privacy.
    
-   It allows users to buy, sell, and exchange crypto all within the app.
