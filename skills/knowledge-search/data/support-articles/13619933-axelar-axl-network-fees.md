# Axelar (AXL) Network – Cross-Chain Communication Made Easy

Axelar enables direct communication and asset movement across different blockchains, making it easy to bridge assets safely.

## How AXL Fees Work

-   **Gas fee** based on **transaction size** and **cross-chain complexity**
    
-   Paid in **AXL tokens**.
    

Edge dynamically estimates fees based on current Axelar network conditions.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – Fastest bridging and transfer
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Best balance
    
-   **Low** – Slower, more economical
    
-   **Custom** – Advanced control over fees
    

---

## Average Cost

Typically **$0.10 to $0.50 USD** depending on the type of cross-chain transfer.

---

## Why It Matters

Cross-chain transfers can be tricky, but Axelar keeps it streamlined. Edge Wallet ensures your fees stay predictable and your assets move quickly across different blockchains.
