# Sui Network – Next-Gen Smart Transactions

Sui is a new Layer 1 blockchain designed for extremely fast throughput, focusing on asset ownership, gaming, and microtransactions.

## How Sui Fees Work

Fees are calculated based on:

-   **Computation resources used**
    
-   **Storage impact**
    
-   **Network load**  
    Fees are paid in **SUI tokens**.
    

Edge estimates the fee dynamically, balancing performance and price based on real-time network conditions.

---

## Fee Options Available

-   **High** – Prioritizes speed
    
-   **Optimal (default)** – Everyday balance
    
-   **Low** – Lower fee, slower settlement
    
-   **Custom** – Advanced users can tweak fees
    

---

## Average Cost

Sui transactions usually cost **$0.002 to $0.01 USD**, depending on transaction complexity.

---

## Why It Matters

Sui’s cutting-edge tech means you can move assets and interact with apps quickly and cheaply. Edge Wallet’s automatic fee adjustment lets you enjoy Sui without worrying about complex fee mechanics.
